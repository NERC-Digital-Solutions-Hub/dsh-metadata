# Light penetration for determination of the euphotic zone in Durleigh Reservoir, 2018

## Overview
- This dataset documents light penetration measurements used to determine the euphotic zone in Durleigh Reservoir during 2018.
- Measurements were collected at multiple depths to profile light availability in the water column.

## Methods and instrumentation
- Instrumentation: HOBO Pendant Temperature/Light 8K Loggers.
- Depths sampled: 0.5 m, 1.5 m, and 2.5 m.
- Location: latitude 51.1210, longitude -3.0403.
- Deployment period: 30 May 2018 to 5 October 2018.
- Measurement cadence: every 10 minutes.
- File naming convention: filenames include the pendant depth, e.g., "0.5mPENDANT.csv".
