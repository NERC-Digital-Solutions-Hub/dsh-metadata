# Elter_T_profiles_2018-2019.txt

## Overview
- Hourly average water temperature measurements at the deepest point of Elterwater Inner basin.
- Time period: 2018-01-01 00:00:00 to 2019-12-31 23:00:00.

## Location
- Coordinates: Latitude 54.4287°, Longitude -3.0350°.

## Measurements and Sampling
- Depths: T at 1 m, 2 m, 3 m, 4 m, 5 m, and 6 m below the surface.
- Sampling cadence: originally 4-minute intervals; hourly averages computed from raw data.
- Sensor: RBRsoloT temperature sensors.
- Accuracy: ± 0.002°C.

## Data Processing
- Raw 4-minute data are hourly averaged.
- Small gaps (< 2 hours) are linearly interpolated.

## Data Structure
- Columns: DateTime (YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM:SS), T_1m (°C), T_2m (°C), T_3m (°C), T_4m (°C), T_5m (°C), T_6m (°C).
