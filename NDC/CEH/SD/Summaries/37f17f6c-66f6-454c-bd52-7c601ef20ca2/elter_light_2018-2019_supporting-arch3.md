# Elter_light_2018-2019.txt

## Overview
- Weekly to monthly vertical profiles of underwater photosynthetically active radiation (PAR) in Elterwater inner basin.
- Timeframe: May 2018 to December 2019.
- Measurement at the deepest point of the basin (coordinates provided).

## Sampling design and timeline
- Profile cadence: weekly to monthly (variable over the study period).
- Depth range for profiles: 0.5 to 6 meters, in half-meter increments.
- Measurements checked for quality via visual inspection.

## Location
- Deepest point of Elterwater inner basin.
- Coordinates: Latitude 54.4287°, Longitude -3.0350°.

## Instrumentation and measurements
- Instrument: LI-COR LI-192 underwater quantum sensor.
- Measured variable: PAR (photosynthetically active radiation).
- Units: Light (μmol s^-1 m^-2).
- Depth resolution: 0.5 m intervals from 0.5 to 6 m.

## Data structure
- Columns: Date (yyyy-mm-dd), Depth (m), Light (μmol s^-1 m^-2).

## Data quality and handling
- Measurements subjected to visual quality check.
