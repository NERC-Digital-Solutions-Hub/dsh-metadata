# Elter_light_2018-2019.txt

## Summary
- Weekly to monthly vertical PAR profiles collected from May 2018 to December 2019.
- Location: deepest point of Elterwater inner basin (Latitude 54.4287, Longitude -3.0350).

## Measurements and instrument
- Parameter: Photosynthetically active radiation (PAR).
- Sensor: LI-COR LI-192 underwater quantum sensor.
- Depth coverage: 0.5 to 6 m, measured at half-meter intervals.
- Data checked via visual inspection.
