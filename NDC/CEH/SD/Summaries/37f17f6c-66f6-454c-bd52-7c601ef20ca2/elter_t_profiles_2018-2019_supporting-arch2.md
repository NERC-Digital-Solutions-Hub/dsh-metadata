# Elter_T_profiles_2018-2019.txt

## Overview
- Hourly average water temperature data for Elterwater Inner Basin's deepest point.
- Time period: 2018-01-01 00:00:00 to 2019-12-31 23:00:00.
- Depths measured: 1 m, 2 m, 3 m, 4 m, 5 m, and 6 m below the surface (4-minute intervals).

## Location and Sensors
- Location coordinates: Lat 54.4287°, Long -3.0350°.
- Sensor type: RBRsoloT temperature sensors.
- Measurement accuracy: ±0.002 °C.

## Data Processing
- Original data: 4-minute intervals.
- Processed to hourly averages.
- Small gaps (< 2 hours) were linearly interpolated.

## Data Structure
- Columns: DateTime (yyyy-mm-dd HH:MM:SS), T_1m (°C), T_2m (°C), T_3m (°C), T_4m (°C), T_5m (°C), T_6m (°C).
