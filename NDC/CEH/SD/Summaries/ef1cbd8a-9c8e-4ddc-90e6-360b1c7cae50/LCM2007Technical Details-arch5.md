# Land Cover Map 2007 (LCM2007) is available in different formats, to suit the requirements of various users.

## Overview
- LCM2007 provides both vector and raster data for the UK, in formats designed to meet diverse user needs.
- Two main data products: vector data (parcels with land cover and derivation metadata) and raster data (five products).

## Vector data
- Most detailed product: polygons representing land cover parcels with attributes and metadata describing derivation (see dataset documentation).
- Minimum mapped unit: 0.5 hectares.
- Supply format: ESRI Shapefile.
- Coverage: UK.

## Raster data
- Five raster products in total.
- 25 x 25 m raster gives the most likely Broad Habitat for each pixel.
- Four freely available 1 x 1 km rasters summarise the 25 x 25 m raster.
- Scale: 25 m and 1 km pixel size.
- Supply format: GeoTiff.
- Coverage: UK.
