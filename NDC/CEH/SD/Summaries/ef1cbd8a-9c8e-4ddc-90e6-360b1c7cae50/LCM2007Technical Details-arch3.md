# Land Cover Map 2007 (LCM2007) is available in different formats, to suit the requirements of various users.

## Overview
- LCM2007 provides land cover information in both vector and raster formats for the UK.
- Designed to meet diverse user needs and analyses.

## Data formats and scales
- Vector data
  - Most detailed product: polygons for land parcels with land cover attributes and metadata on derivation.
  - Minimum mapable unit: 0.5 ha.
  - Format: ESRI Shapefile.
  - Coverage: UK.
- Raster data
  - Five raster products.
  - 25 x 25 m raster: most likely Broad Habitat per pixel.
  - Four freely available 1 x 1 km rasters summarise the 25 x 25 m raster.
  - Detailed metadata in Land Cover Map 2007 Dataset Documentation.
  - Scales: 25 m and 1 km pixel size.
  - Format: GeoTiff.
  - Coverage: UK.
