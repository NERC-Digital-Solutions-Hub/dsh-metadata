# Land Cover Map 2007 (LCM2007) is available in different formats, to suit the requirements of various users.

- Purpose and formats
  - LCM2007 provides map-based data products in both vector and raster formats to suit different user needs.
  - Vector data is the most detailed product, with each polygon representing a land cover parcel and attributes describing land cover plus metadata on how it was derived (see Dataset Documentation).

- Vector data
  - Minimum Mapable Unit: 0.5 hectares
  - Supply Format: ESRI Shapefile
  - Coverage: UK

- Raster data
  - There are five raster products; a 25 x 25 m raster gives the most likely Broad Habitat for each pixel.
  - Four freely available 1 x 1 km raster products summarize the 25 x 25 m raster.
  - Scale/Pixel sizes: 25 m and 1 km
  - Supply Format: GeoTiff
  - Coverage: UK
