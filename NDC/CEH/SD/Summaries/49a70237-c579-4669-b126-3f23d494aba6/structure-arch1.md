# Details of data structure

- The dataset comprises 18 documents in 10 RTF and 8 CSV formats, each containing transcripts of interviews conducted in one of the study villages.
- Interviewee names are kept anonymous.
- Documents are organized into four exercise categories:
  - Seasonal_calendar: 3 documents
  - Trend_analysis: 7 documents
  - Village_profiles: 1 document
  - Wealthranking_qual: 7 documents
- Data were collected during fieldwork in Mabalane with 11 villages visited during a two-week pre-visit in July 2014.
- All data were recorded in Portuguese and English; English transcripts were compiled after fieldwork.
- Translations were performed by a translator (Portuguese - Changana) alongside a research team, with notes taken in Portuguese/English.

## Content details by section

- Seasonal_calendar: documents reflect an exercise focusing on seasonal patterns, conducted with village participants and translated/transcribed afterward.
- Trend_analysis: multiple documents capturing trend-related discussions, following the same field protocol and translation/transcription process.
- Village_profiles: a single document summarizing profiles from interviews with village leaders or representatives collected during the pre-visit.
- Wealthranking_qual: documents contain qualitative information recorded during wealth rankings, with data captured in Portuguese and English transcripts.
