# Details of data structure

- Dataset composition
  - 10 RTF and 8 CSV documents
  - Each document contains transcripts of interviews conducted in one of the villages
  - Interviewee names are anonymized

- Data category and document counts
  - Seasonal_calendar: 3 documents
  - Trend_analysis: 7 documents
  - Village_profiles: 1 document
  - Wealthranking_qual: 7 documents

- Data collection and transcription workflow
  - Seasonal_calendar
    - Led by a researcher and a translator (Portuguese - Changana) with 3–8 villagers per village
    - Field protocol followed
    - Notes in Portuguese/English
    - English transcripts compiled after fieldwork
  - Trend_analysis
    - Led by the lead researcher and a translator (Portuguese - Changana) with 3–8 villagers per village
    - Field protocol followed
    - Notes in Portuguese/English
    - English transcripts compiled after fieldwork
  - Village_profiles
    - Two-week pre-visit in July 2014
    - 11 villages visited; interviews with village leaders or representatives
    - Data recorded in Portuguese and English; transcripts compiled after fieldwork
  - Wealthranking_qual
    - Qualitative information from wealth rankings conducted in each study village
    - Data recorded in Portuguese and English; transcripts compiled after fieldwork

- Language and transcription details
  - Field notes and transcripts involve Portuguese and English
  - English transcripts are compiled after the fieldwork phase

- Data governance and provenance considerations
  - Data are organized by study exercise (seasonal calendar, trend analysis, village profiles, wealth ranking)
  - Anonymization of interviewees helps protect privacy
  - Documentation implies alignment with field protocols and post-fieldwork transcription to ensure usability and discoverability

- Implications for data stewardship
  - Ensure consistent metadata for each document type and village
  - Maintain the anonymization scheme and track any re-identification risks
  - Support integration of RTF and CSV formats into portals and catalogs
  - Preserve language provenance (Portuguese/English) and transcription workflows for traceability and reuse
