# Details of data structure

- Dataset composition
  - 10 RTF documents and 8 CSV documents containing interview transcripts.
  - Each document contains transcripts from interviews conducted in one village.
  - Interviewee names are anonymized.

- Thematic categories and counts
  - Seasonal_calendar: 3 documents
  - Trend_analysis: 7 documents
  - Village_profiles: 1 document
  - Wealthranking_qual: 7 documents

- Fieldwork and collection details
  - Seasonal_calendar and Trend_analysis led by a researcher (and a translator; Portuguese-Changana) with groups of 3â€“8 villagers in three study villages (seasonal calendar) or in each study village (trend analysis).
  - Village_profiles based on a two-week pre-visit in July 2014; 11 villages in Mabalane visited; interviews with village leaders or representatives.
  - Data collected in Portuguese and English; English transcripts compiled after field work.

- Language and translation
  - Transcripts recorded in Portuguese and English.
  - Translation involvement included Portuguese to English and a Portuguese-Changana translator.

- Data processing and anonymization
  - Transcripts compiled after field work.
  - Interviewee names are kept anonymous.

- Geographic and scope notes relevant for GIS
  - Villages are the primary spatial units; data is organized by village and theme.
  - The dataset provides qualitative information linked to village-level contexts, suitable for informing spatial analyses or map-based products when linked to separate geospatial village data (e.g., boundaries, locations).

- Temporal context
  - Fieldwork and data collection occurred around July 2014 (Village_profiles pre-visit in July 2014).
  - Transcripts compiled after fieldwork, with sequencing by thematic category.
