# THAMES CATHCMENT

## Overview
- A map illustrating pharmaceutical concentration sampling points within the Thames catchment.

## Geographic coverage
- Spans multiple sub-catchments and rivers, including Cherwell, Windrush, Thames, Churn, Evenlode, Ray, Cole, Leach, Glyme, Ock, Kennet, Loddon, Whitewater, Blackwater, Wey, Wye, Misbourne, Ver, Gade, Colne, Mole, and Tilling Bourne.
- Indicates a broad network of sampling locations across the Thames basin.

## Sampling sites
- Sampling points labeled with codes such as TC1, TC2, TC3, TC4, TC5, TC6, TC7, TC8, TC9, TC10, TC11, TC12, TC13, TC14, TC15, TC16, TC17, TC18, TC19, TC20, TC21.
- Site names appear in conjunction with various rivers and tributaries, marking discrete pharmaceutical concentration sampling locations.

## Data provenance and copyright
- Source: Thames catchment pharmaceutical concentration sampling data (NERC CEH).

## Map features
- Includes a scale (0 to 40 km) and a north indicator (N), suggesting geospatial positioning for local to regional interpretation.
