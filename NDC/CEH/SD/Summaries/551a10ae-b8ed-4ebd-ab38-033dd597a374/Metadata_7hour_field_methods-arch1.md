# SAMPLING METHODS

- Overview
  - Two sampling streams: rainfall and stream water.
  - Timeframe for both: 6-Mar-07 to 27-Jan-09.
  - Sampling interval for both: every 7 hours.
  - Filtration performed in the lab.

- Rainfall
  - Collection method: Bulk sample.
  - Collection device: Continuously open, 57.5 cm diameter plastic collector with anti-bird protection; 24 Ã— 250 ml polypropylene bottles for automated sampling.
  - Flow/volume measurement: Rainfall amounts from a 12.5 cm diameter ground-level tipping bucket raingauge at the Carreg Wen automatic weather station.
  - Start/finish: 6-Mar-07 to 27-Jan-09.
  - Filtration: Lab.

- Stream water
  - Collection method: Spot samples.
  - Collection device: Xian automatic sampler with 24 bottles programmed to collect 500 ml samples at 7 hourly intervals into high-density polypropylene bottles.
  - Flow/volume measurement: CEH stream (flume) gauges used according to the hydrological network.
  - Start/finish: 6-Mar-07 to 27-Jan-09.
  - Filtration: Lab.
