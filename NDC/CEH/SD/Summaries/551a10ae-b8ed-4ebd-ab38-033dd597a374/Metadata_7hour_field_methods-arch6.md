# SAMPLING METHODS

- Timeframe: 6-Mar-2007 to 27-Jan-2009
- Scope: Rainfall (bulk samples) and stream water (spot samples) collected at 7 hourly intervals
- Filtration: Performed in the laboratory

Rainfall
- COLLECTION METHOD: Bulk sample
- COLLECTION DEVICE: Continuously open, 57.5 cm diameter plastic collector with anti-bird protection; 24 Ã— 250 ml polypropylene bottles for automated sampling
- FLOW/VOLUME: Rainfall amounts from 12.5 cm diameter ground-level tipping bucket raingauge at Carreg Wen automatic weather station
- SAMPLING INTERVAL: 7 hourly
- START/FINISH: 6-Mar-2007 to 27-Jan-2009
- NOTES: Lab filtration applied

Stream water
- COLLECTION METHOD: Spot samples
- COLLECTION DEVICE: Xian automatic sampler with 24 bottles programmed to collect 500 ml samples at 7 hourly intervals into high density polypropylene bottles
- FLOW/VOLUME: CEH stream (flume) gauges used according to hydrological network
- SAMPLING INTERVAL: 7 hourly
- START/FINISH: 6-Mar-2007 to 27-Jan-2009
- NOTES: Lab filtration applied
