# SAMPLING METHODS

- Timeframe: 6-Mar-2007 to 27-Jan-2009 for both rainfall and stream water.
- SAMPLING INTERVAL: 7 hourly for both rainfall and stream water.

- Rainfall
  - COLLECTION METHOD: Bulk sample.
  - COLLECTION DEVICE: Continuously open, 57.5 cm diameter plastic collector with anti-bird protection; 24 x 250 ml polypropylene bottles for automated sample collection.
  - FLOW/VOLUME: Rainfall amounts from 12.5 cm diameter ground-level tipping bucket raingauge at Carreg Wen automatic weather station.
  - FILTRATION: Lab.

- Stream water
  - COLLECTION METHOD: Spot samples.
  - COLLECTION DEVICE: Xian automatic sampler with 24 bottles programmed to collect 500 ml samples at 7 hourly intervals into high density polypropylene bottles.
  - FLOW/VOLUME: CEH stream (flume) gauges used according to hydrological network.
  - FILTRATION: Lab.
