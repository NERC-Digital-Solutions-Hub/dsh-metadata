# SAMPLING METHODS

## Purpose and Scope
- Documents sampling protocols for rainfall and stream water used in environmental monitoring.
- Sampling cadence: 7 hourly intervals for both rainfall and stream water.
- Time period: 6-Mar-2007 to 27-Jan-2009.
- Filtration: Samples processed in lab.

## Rainfall Sampling
- Collection method: Bulk sample.
- Collection device: Continuously open, 57.5 cm diameter plastic collector with bird protection; 24 x 250 mL polypropylene bottles for automated sampling.
- Flow/Volume: Rainfall amounts from 12.5 cm diameter ground-level tipping bucket raingauge at Carreg Wen automatic weather station.
- Start/Finish: 6-Mar-2007 to 27-Jan-2009.
- Filtration: Lab.

## Stream Water Sampling
- Collection method: Spot samples.
- Collection device: Xian automatic sampler with 24 bottles programmed to collect 500 mL samples at 7 hourly intervals into high-density polypropylene bottles.
- Flow/Volume: CEH stream (flume) gauges used according to hydrological network.
- Start/Finish: 6-Mar-2007 to 27-Jan-2009.
- Filtration: Lab.
