# Type and Condition Codes

- Purpose
  - Defines standardized codes for asset type and condition to support data collection, cleaning, and analysis.

- Asset domain 1: Transportation routes
  - Type codes
    - 1 Major (paved)
    - 2 Major (unpaved)
    - 3 Minor (paved)
    - 4 Minor (unpaved)
    - 5 Track (unpaved)
  - Condition codes
    - 1 Intact
    - 2 Obstructed/damaged

- Asset domain 2: Settlement/infrastructure
  - Type codes
    - 1 Residential (permanent)
    - 2 Residential (informal/temporary)
    - 3 Industrial i.e. hydropower
    - 4 Municipal
  - Condition codes
    - 1 Intact
    - 2 Obstructed/damaged

- Practical use for analysts
  - Enables consistent categorization across datasets
  - Facilitates cross-dataset unification, quality assurance, and modelling of infrastructure status and potential impacts on accessibility or service delivery
