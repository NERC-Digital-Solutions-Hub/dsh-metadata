# Supporting Document Data from a meteorological station next to Esthwaite Water 2012, 2013, 2014 and 2015. Jones, I.D., Feuchtmayr, H., Maberly, S.C. (2017)

- Location and period
  - Meteorological station atop a boat house adjacent to Esthwaite Water, Cumbria, England.
  - Data span from 2012 to 2015.
  - Times recorded in GMT (UTC).

- Variables and units
  - Date_GMT: Date and time of measurement (GMT)
  - Air_Temperature: Air temperature in degrees Celsius
  - Wind_Speed: Wind speed in metres per second

- Sampling regime
  - Measurements collected every 4 minutes.
  - Hourly averages provided, calculated over the previous hour (e.g., the 2 p.m. value averages data from 1–2 p.m., including 2 p.m., excluding 1 p.m.).

- Quality control and data quality
  - Data are raw and not yet calibrated or quality controlled.
  - Visual checks performed; obvious hardware-related errors removed.
  - Gaps occur due to maintenance or sensor/logger problems.

- Data structure
  - File format: CSV
  - Columns:
    - Date_GMT (Date and time in GMT)
    - Air_Temperature (°C)
    - Wind_Speed (m/s)

- Usage context
  - Data are used in several current scientific studies, including PhD projects.
  - Suitable for analyses of correlations, patterns, and predictive modeling after appropriate calibration and cleaning.
