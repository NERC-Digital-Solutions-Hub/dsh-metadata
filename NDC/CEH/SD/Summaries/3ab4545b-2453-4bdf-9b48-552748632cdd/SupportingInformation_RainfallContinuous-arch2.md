# LOCAR BASELINE DATASETS

## Overview
- Part of the Lowland Catchment Research (LOCAR) Programme; continuous rainfall gauges installed at 6 sites across the Frome/Piddle, Pang/Lambourn, and Tern catchments.
- Data collection spanned 2002â€“2007, with differing periods by site.

## Measured variable
- Rainfall (millimetres, mm)
- Measurement frequency: every 15 minutes

## Locations (sites and catchments)
- Higher Came Farm, FP13, RNGA
- Lower Wraxall Farm, FP35, RNGA
- Grimsbury Wood (Hermitage), PL14, RNGA
- Highfield Farm, PL16, RNGA
- West Ilsley, PL29, RNGA
- Bowling Green, TE10, RNGA
- Catchment codes: FP = Frome/Piddle; PL = Pang/Lambourn; TE = Tern
- RNGA = Rain gauge

## Instrumentation and data handling
- Automatic tipping bucket rain gauges
- Pre-set amount of precipitation triggers a tip; tips converted to 15-minute rainfall data values
- Data logged on connected loggers
- Catchment Service Team responsible for installation, maintenance, and downloading loggers

## Data quality
- Data are raw and have not been quality controlled
