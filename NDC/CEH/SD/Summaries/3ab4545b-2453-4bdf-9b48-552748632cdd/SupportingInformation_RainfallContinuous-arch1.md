# LOCAR BASELINE DATASETS LOCAR

## Overview
- Continuous rainfall data collected at 6 sites within the Frome/Piddle, Pang/Lambourn, and Tern catchments as part of the LOCAR Programme.
- Timeframe: data collected between November 2002 and May 2007, with differing durations per site.
- Resolution: rainfall recorded at 15-minute intervals.

## Data specifics
- Variable measured: Rainfall (mm), with a 15-minute measurement frequency.
- Instrumentation: Automatic tipping bucket rain gauges using a funnel and seesaw mechanism; each tip is converted into a 15-minute rainfall value and stored on a logger.
- Data quality: Raw data not quality controlled.

## Sites (Site Code – Site Name – Catchment)
- FP13 – Higher Came Farm – Frome/Piddle Catchment
- FP35 – Lower Wraxall Farm – Frome/Piddle Catchment
- PL14 – Grimsbury Wood (Hermitage) – Pang/Lambourn Catchment
- PL16 – Highfield Farm – Pang/Lambourn Catchment
- PL29 – West Ilsley – Pang/Lambourn Catchment
- TE10 – Bowling Green – Tern Catchment

## Data collection and responsibilities
- Data collection and maintenance carried out by the Catchment Service Team in each catchment; responsible for installing, maintaining, and downloading loggers at each site.
