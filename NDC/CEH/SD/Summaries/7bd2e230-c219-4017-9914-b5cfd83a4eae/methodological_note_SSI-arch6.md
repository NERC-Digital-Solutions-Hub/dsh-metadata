# Semi-structured interviews Methodological note of data produced

## Objectives
- Characterize and quantify production losses due to conflict.
- Assess the relation between areas of conflict and environmental variables/landscape features.
- Identify mitigation measures used.

## Sample
- Planned larger sample, but logistic constraints limited to 29 people from 3 communities.
- Interview duration: 25–50 minutes.
- Interviews randomly selected during field work; conducted by one or two team members, as privately as possible.

## Resources needed
- Tablet with ODK questionnaire.
- 30 pebbles for appraisal of production and losses (crops and livestock).

## Methods
- Interviewees randomly selected during field work in the communities.
- One or two team members administered the questionnaire, aiming for privacy.
