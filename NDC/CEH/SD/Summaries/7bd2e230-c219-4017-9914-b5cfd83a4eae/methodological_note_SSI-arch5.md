# Semi-structured interviews Methodological note of data produced

- Objectives
  - Characterize and quantify production losses due to conflict
  - Assess the relation between areas of conflict and environmental variables/landscape features
  - Identify mitigation measures used

- Sample
  - 29 participants from 3 communities (reduced from planned larger sample due to logistical constraints)
  - Interview duration: 25–50 minutes

- Resources needed
  - Tablet with ODK questionnaire
  - 30 pebbles for appraisal of production and losses (crops and livestock)

- Methods
  - Interviews randomly selected during fieldwork in the communities
  - One or two team members administered the questionnaire, aiming for privacy

- Digital data produced
  - a) CSV file with the questionnaire (ready for conversion to ODK format) – 1 CSV, in Portuguese
  - b) Summary CSV file with all data – 1 CSV, in English

- Data governance notes (implications for stewardship)
  - Data formats are CSVs with multilingual outputs (Portuguese questionnaire; English summary)
  - Questionnaire data is prepared for downstream ODK conversion
  - Interviews were conducted privately, supporting respondent confidentiality
  - Documentation includes the scope of objectives, sample constraints, and data for potential sharing and reuse
