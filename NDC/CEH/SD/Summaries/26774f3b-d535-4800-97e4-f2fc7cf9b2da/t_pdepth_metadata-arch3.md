# High-resolution temperature and precipitation measurements for fire-affected locations in Australia (2019-2020) and Tenerife (2020-2021)

## Overview
- High-resolution environmental monitoring data focusing on temperature and precipitation depth in fire-affected locations.
- Intended to support understanding of runoff occurrence and inform monitoring frameworks and policy decisions.

## Data files
- T_Pdepth_ma.csv
- T_Pdepth_ts.csv

## What, Where, When, How, Why, Who

- What
  - Temperature and precipitation depth recorded at 10-minute intervals.
- Where
  - Madre del Agua, Tenerife, Spain (UTM 28N 28 R 343340 3119657)
  - Thompson reservoir, Victoria, Australia (UTM 55 H 441956 5822296)
- When
  - Madre del Agua: 17/11/20 to 19/11/2021
  - Thompson reservoir: 28/03/19 to 13/01/2020
- How
  - RainWise Rainew raingauge coupled to Onset HOBO pendant datalogger (UA-003-64)
- Why
  - To monitor environmental parameters related to runoff occurrence
- Who
  - Dr Jonay Neris Tome, Dr Carmen Sánchez-García, Dr Cristina Santín, Prof. Stefan Doerr, Dr Gary Sheridan

## Data characteristics

- Completeness: Full record
- Features: Date and time (dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm), temperature (°C), precipitation depth (mm)
- Treatments/Replication/Controls: Not applicable (NA)
- Periodicity: 10 minutes
- Observations: Madre del Agua 9,723; Thompson 10,050

## Location details and data provenance

- Madre del Agua (Tenerife, Spain) coordinates: UTMs 28N 28R 343340 3119657
- Thompson reservoir (Victoria, Australia) coordinates: UTMs 55H 441956 5822296
- Data collection instrument: RainWise Rainew rain gauge with Onset HOBO pendant logger UA-003-64

## Implications for monitoring frameworks

- Demonstrates high-resolution, time-stamped environmental data collection in fire-affected contexts.
- Highlights the importance of metadata availability (coordinates, timing, instrument, data format) for data reuse and governance.
- Provides a clear example of data completeness and standardized reporting (variables, units, time steps), useful for designing monitoring dashboards and data-sharing policies.
