# High-resolution temperature and precipitation measurements for fire-affected locations in Australia (2019-2020) and Tenerife (2020-2021)

## Dataset Overview
- High-resolution (10-minute intervals) measurements of temperature and precipitation for two fire-affected areas.
- Temporal coverage:
  - Madre del Agua (Tenerife, Spain): 17/11/20 to 19/11/2021
  - Thompson reservoir (Victoria, Australia): 28/03/19 to 13/01/2020

## Data Files
- T_Pdepth_ma.csv — Madre del Agua (Tenerife)
- T_Pdepth_ts.csv — Thompson reservoir (Australia)
- Each file includes:
  - Date and time (dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm)
  - Temperature (Temp: °C)
  - Precipitation depth (P: mm)

## Locations and Coordinates
- Madre del Agua, Tenerife, Spain — UTM 28N 28R 343340 3119657
- Thompson reservoir, Victoria, Australia — UTM 55 H 441956 5822296

## Data Collection and Methods
- Instrumentation: RainWise rain gauge coupled to Onset HOBO pendant datalogger (UA-003-64)
- Periodicity: 10-minute sampling
- Completeness: Full record

## Purpose and Contributors
