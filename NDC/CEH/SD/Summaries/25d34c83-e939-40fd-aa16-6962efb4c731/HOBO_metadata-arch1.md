# Water temperature measurements from Durleigh Reservoir 2018 - HOBO TidbiT

## Overview
- Metadata for water temperature measurements at Durleigh Reservoir in 2018.
- Measurements collected using HOBO TidbiT v2 loggers.

## Spatial and measurement setup
- Location: Latitude 51.1215, Longitude -3.0445.
- Temperature chain comprises five thermistors at depths from surface (0 m) to bottom (4 m), at 1 m intervals.

## Temporal coverage and sampling
- Measurement period: 02/22/2018 to 10/05/2018.
- Data recorded at 10-minute intervals.

## Data format and units
- Temperature reported in degrees Fahrenheit (oF).
- Date/time format: mm/dd/yyyy HH:MM:ss.

## Instrument and deployment
- Thermistors: HOBO TidbiT v2 loggers (Onset).
- Deployed by Emily Slavin and Danielle Wain.
