# Water temperature measurements from Durleigh Reservoir 2018 - RBR SoloT

## Overview
- Water temperature measurements from Durleigh Reservoir in 2018 using an RBR SoloT thermistor chain.
- Temperature data collected at 1 m depth intervals from 0 m (surface) to 6 m depth.
- Sampling cadence: every 10 minutes.

## Dataset details
- Location: latitude 51.1210, longitude -3.0403
- Depths recorded: 0 m, 1 m, 2 m, 3 m, 4 m, 5 m, 6 m
- Time period: 22/02/2018 to 05/10/2018
- Date/time format: dd/mm/yyyy HH:MM
- Temperature units: degrees Celsius (Â°C)
- Documentation: temperature metadata file accompanies the dataset

## Data quality and gaps
- Data missing for depths 1 m, 2 m, and 3 m due to corrupted files on download from the thermistors
- Indicates a data quality gap that may require re-download or remediation
