# Water temperature measurements from Durleigh Reservoir 2018 - HOBO TidbiT

## Overview
- Metadata describing water temperature measurements taken at Durleigh Reservoir in 2018.
- Five HOBO TidbiT v2 thermistors positioned on a depth chain from surface (0 m) to 4 m depth.
- Temperatures recorded at 10-minute intervals over 22 February 2018 to 5 October 2018.
- Temperatures reported in Fahrenheit (oF).
- Deployment and authors: temperature chain deployed by Emily Slavin and Danielle Wain.
- Dataset citation: Slavin, E.I., Wain, D.J. 2018. Water temperature measurements from Durleigh Reservoir 2018.
