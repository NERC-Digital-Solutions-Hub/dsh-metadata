# Water temperature measurements from Durleigh Reservoir 2018 - HOBO TidbiT

## Overview
- Document describes a dataset of water temperature measurements at Durleigh Reservoir using HOBO TidbiT loggers in 2018.
- Data are intended to support environmental monitoring, transparency, and future decision-making.

## Data collection details
- Five thermistors arranged on a temperature chain from surface (0 m) to bottom (4 m) at 1 m depth intervals.
- Temperature measurements recorded at 10-minute intervals.
- Sampling period: 02/22/2018 to 10/05/2018.
- Location: Latitude 51.1215, Longitude -3.0445.
- Temperature units: Fahrenheit (oF).

## Instrumentation and deployment
- Thermistors: HOBO TidbiT v2 loggers (Onset).
- Deployed by: Emily Slavin and Danielle Wain.

## Metadata, provenance, and citation
- Metadata: Temperature metadata file for Durleigh Reservoir (2018) is included.
