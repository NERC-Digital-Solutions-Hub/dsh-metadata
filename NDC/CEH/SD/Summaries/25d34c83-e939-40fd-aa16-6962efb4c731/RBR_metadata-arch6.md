# Water temperature measurements from Durleigh Reservoir 2018 - RBR SoloT

## Overview
- Water temperature measurements from Durleigh Reservoir in 2018 using RBR SoloT thermistors.
- Temperature chain spans from surface (0 m) to bottom (6 m) in 1 m depth intervals.
- Temperatures reported in degrees Celsius (°C).

## Location and Instrument
- Location coordinates: latitude 51.1210, longitude -3.0403.
- Instrument: RBR SoloT thermistors arranged along a vertical depth chain (0–6 m).

## Data Details
- Measurements at 10-minute intervals.
- Timeframe: 22/02/2018 to 05/10/2018.
- Date/time format: dd/mm/yyyy HH:MM.
- All temperatures in oC (degrees Celsius).

## Data Gaps and Quality
- Data missing for thermistors at 1 m, 2 m, and 3 m depths due to corrupted files during download from the thermistors.
