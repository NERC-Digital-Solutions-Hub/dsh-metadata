# Water temperature measurements from Durleigh Reservoir 2018 - RBR SoloT

## Overview
- Temperature measurements from Durleigh Reservoir in 2018 using RBR SoloT thermistors.
- Depths from surface (0 m) to bottom (6 m) at 1 m intervals.
- Measurements every 10 minutes between 22/02/2018 and 05/10/2018.
- Temperatures reported in degrees Celsius (°C).
- Location: latitude 51.1210, longitude -3.0403.
- Data accompanied by a temperature metadata file.

## Data Collection Details
- Instrumentation: RBR SoloT thermistors arranged along a depth chain (0–6 m, at 1 m steps).
- Deployment: by Emily Slavin and Danielle Wain.
- Time frame and cadence: 22/02/2018 to 05/10/2018, 10-minute intervals.

## Data Quality and Gaps
- Data missing for depths 1 m, 2 m, and 3 m due to corrupted files on download from the thermistors.
- All other depths' data are maintained for the stated period.

## Metadata and Citation
- Metadata file describing the temperature measurements.
- Citation: Slavin, E.I., Wain, D.J. 2018. Water temperature measurements from Durleigh Reservoir 2018.
