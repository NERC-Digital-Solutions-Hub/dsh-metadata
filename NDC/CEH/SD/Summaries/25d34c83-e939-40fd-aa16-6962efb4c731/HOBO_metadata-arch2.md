# Water temperature measurements from Durleigh Reservoir 2018 - HOBO TidbiT

## Overview
- Metadata file describing water temperature measurements at Durleigh Reservoir for 2018.
- Data collected using a temperature chain spanning the surface to bottom of the water column.

## Location and instrumentation
- Location: Latitude 51.1215, Longitude -3.0445
- Five thermistors positioned at 0 m to 4 m depth (0, 1, 2, 3, 4 m)
- Instruments: HOBO TidbiT v2 loggers (Onset)
- Deployment conducted by Emily Slavin and Danielle Wain

## Temporal coverage and data format
- Sampling interval: 10 minutes
- Period: 22/02/2018 to 05/10/2018 (dates given in dd/mm/yyyy format)
- Date/time format in dataset: mm/dd/yyyy HH:MM:ss
- Temperature unit: degrees Fahrenheit (oF)

## Data management and citation
- Dataset citation: Slavin, E.I., Wain, D.J. 2018. Water temperature measurements from Durleigh Reservoir 2018.
- Metadata provides standardized fields (location, depths, instrumentation, interval, format, units) to support data quality, transformation, and reuse.
