# Headwater stream macrophyte data 2007 [Countryside Survey] Dataset description

## Overview
- This dataset describes headwater stream macrophytes from the Countryside Survey 2007.
- Collected under the Mean Trophic Rank (MTR) protocol, with an expanded plant species checklist beyond typical MTR needs.
- Data was recorded for 100 m stream reaches, using tablet computers and custom software.

## Data Collection and Methods
- Survey method: Standard MTR protocol (Holmes et al. 1999) to record presence and extent of macrophytes in a 100 m reach.
- Expanded species checklist used to capture additional plant information.
- Data capture: Occurrence and abundance recorded on tablets with custom software.

## Data Table and Fields
- Table: STREAM_MACROPHYTES_07.csv
- Key fields:
  - SQUARE: 1 km square sampling site code
  - YEAR: Year of survey (CS Yr)
  - SITE_ID: Site identifier within the square
  - SURVEY_DATE: Date of survey
  - PLANT_NAME: Scientific name of plant
  - PROPORTION: Proportion (%) of the 100 m stretch covered
  - LC07 / LC07_NUM: Land class 2007 (text and numeric code)
  - EZ_DESC_07: Environmental zone description
  - COUNTY07: County or equivalent
  - COUNTRY: England, Scotland, or Wales
