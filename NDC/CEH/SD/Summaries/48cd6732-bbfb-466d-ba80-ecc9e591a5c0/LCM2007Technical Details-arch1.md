Land Cover Map 2007 (LCM2007) is available in different formats, to suit the requirements of various users.

## Overview
- LCM2007 provides both vector and raster formats to accommodate different analytical needs.
- Coverage: United Kingdom (UK).

## Vector data
- Most detailed product: each polygon represents a land cover parcel with attributes describing land cover and metadata on derivation.
- Minimum Mapable Unit: 0.5 hectares.
- Supply Format: ESRI Shapefile.
- Coverage: UK.

## Raster data
- Five raster products in total.
- One 25 x 25 meter raster indicating the most likely Broad Habitat for each pixel.
- Four freely available 1 x 1 km rasters that summarize the 25 m raster.
- Scales: 25 m and 1 km pixel sizes.
- Supply Format: GeoTiff.
- Coverage: UK.
