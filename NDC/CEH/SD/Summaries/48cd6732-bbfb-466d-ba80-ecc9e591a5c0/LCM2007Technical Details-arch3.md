# Land Cover Map 2007 (LCM2007) is available in different formats, to suit the requirements of various users.

- Purpose and scope
  - Provides land cover information in both vector and raster formats to support a range of user needs.
  - Detailed metadata and attributes available to aid interpretation and reuse.
  - UK coverage.

- Vector data
  - Most detailed product: polygons representing land cover parcels with descriptive attributes and metadata on derivation.
  - Scale/precision: Minimum mapable unit is 0.5 hectares.
  - Supply format: ESRI Shapefile.

- Raster data
  - Five raster products in total.
  - A 25 x 25 m raster indicates the most likely Broad Habitat per pixel.
  - Four freely available 1 x 1 km rasters summarize the 25 x 25 m data.
  - Scale/precision: 25 m and 1 km pixel sizes.
  - Supply format: GeoTiff.
