# METADATA FOR 'LOCH LEVEN WATERFOWL 1968-2007' DATASET

## Overview
- Dataset of counts for 10 waterfowl species at Loch Leven from 1968 to 2007.
- Part of a long-term lake monitoring programme conducted by Scottish Natural Heritage (SNH) and predecessor bodies.
- Counts represent the annual peak count derived from monthly counts from September through March (spanning the year indicated to the following March).

## Species Included
- Coot
- Cormorant
- Great Crested Grebe
- Greylag Goose
- Mallard
- Mute Swan
- Pink-footed Goose
- Pochard
- Teal
- Tufted Duck

## Sampling Regime and Collection Methods
- Monthly autumn and winter counts collected from land-based vantage points.
- 1967â€“1994: monthly counts conducted by SNH; 1995 onward: monthly counts following Wetland Bird Survey (WeBS) methods.
- From 2003: surveys conducted fortnightly; from 2008: SNH collaboration with RSPB Loch Leven.
- Some gaps exist where monthly counts were not completed.
- Peak counts for Pink-footed Goose are dawn roost counts rather than standard WeBS counts.

## Data Quality Control
- Quality controlled by Neil Mitchell (and predecessors at SNH) since 2009.

## Data Structure
- Columns: year, species, count, units.
