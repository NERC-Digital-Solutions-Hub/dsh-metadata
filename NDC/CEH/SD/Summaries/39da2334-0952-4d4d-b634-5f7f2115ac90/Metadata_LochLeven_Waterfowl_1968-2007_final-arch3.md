# METADATA FOR 'LOCH LEVEN WATERFOWL 1968-2007' DATASET

- Citation: Mitchell, N., Brooks P., Squire, J., Wright, G. & Allison, A. Loch Leven Waterfowl 1968-2007. Scottish Natural Heritage.
- Data contact: Reserve Warden, Loch Leven National Nature Reserve, The Pier, Loch Leven, Kinross, KY13 8UF. Tel: 01577 864439.

## Nature and units of recorded values
- Counts of 10 waterfowl species collected from Loch Leven (1968–2007) by SNH and predecessor bodies as part of long-term lake monitoring.
- Annual peak counts derived from monthly counts recorded from September of the year through March of the following year.
- Species (10): Coot, Cormorant, Great Crested Grebe, Greylag Goose, Mallard, Mute Swan, Pink-footed Goose, Pochard, Teal, Tufted Duck.

## Quality control
- Quality controlled by Neil Mitchell (since 2009) and predecessors (SNH Reserve Wardens).

## Sampling regime & collection methods
- Monthly autumn and winter counts from land-based vantage points.
- 1967–1994: monthly counts by SNH.
- Since 1995: monthly counts using Wetland Bird Survey (WeBS) methods.
- Since 2003: surveys conducted fortnightly.
- Since 2008: SNH collaboration with RSPB Loch Leven.
- Gaps exist where some monthly counts were not completed.
- Peak Pink-footed Goose counts are dawn roost counts (not standard WeBS counts).

## Data structure
- Columns: year, species, count, units.
