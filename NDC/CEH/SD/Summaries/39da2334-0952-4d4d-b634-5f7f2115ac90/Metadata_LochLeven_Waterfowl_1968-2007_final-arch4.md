# METADATA FOR 'LOCH LEVEN WATERFOWL 1968-2007' DATASET

- The dataset provides counts of 10 waterfowl species at Loch Leven from 1968 to 2007 as part of a long-term monitoring programme by Scottish Natural Heritage (SNH) and its predecessors.
- Counts represent annual peak numbers derived from monthly counts collected from September of the indicated year through March of the following year, for the whole loch.

## Data scope and content
- Species counted: Coot, Cormorant, Great Crested Grebe, Greylag Goose, Mallard, Mute Swan, Pink-footed Goose, Pochard, Teal, Tufted Duck.
- Data structure: columns for year, species, count, and units.

## Collection methods and sampling regime
- Data collected via land-based counts during autumn and winter (September–March).
- Collection history:
  - 1967–1994: monthly counts by SNH (and predecessors).
  - 1995 onward: monthly counts using Wetland Bird Survey (WeBS) methods.
  - 2003 onward: surveys conducted fortnightly.
  2008 onward: SNH collaborates with RSPB Loch Leven for counts.
- Gaps: some months lack completed counts.
- Peak counts for Pink-footed Goose are dawn roost counts, not standard WeBS counts.

## Data structure and references
- Dataset includes a units field; counts are expressed as numbers of individuals.
- Reference: Austin, G.E., Collier, M.P. & Rehfisch, M.M. (2008) Non-estuarine Coastal Waterbird Survey: Population estimates and broad comparisons with previous surveys. BTO Research Report No. 501.
