# United Kingdom Butterfly Monitoring Scheme: site indices data 2015

## Summary
- The original 2015 site indices dataset contained data issues:
  - Missing data for Polyommatus bellargus and Adonis Blue.
  - Data for Polyommatus icarus and Common Blue were corrupted and not shown under the correct species name.
- A corrected version, Version 2, has been created and supersedes the original dataset.

## Corrected version details
- New dataset title: United Kingdom Butterfly Monitoring Scheme: site indices data 2015 version 2
- Authors: Botham, M.; Roy, D.; Brereton, T.; Middlebrook, I.
- Year: 2016
- Repository: NERC Environmental Information Data Centre
- DOI: https://doi.org/10.5285/cec0fd19-688b-4e3e-8332-cf6506bb4612

## Data governance and accessibility
- The corrected dataset replaces the flawed 2015 version, ensuring data integrity and reliability for monitoring assessments.
- Public availability and a formal citation (with DOI) support transparency and traceability of the corrected data.

## Relevance for monitoring framework authors
- Demonstrates the importance of rigorous data quality checks, versioning, and clear communication when datasets are corrected.
- Highlights the need for accessible metadata, provenance, and governance to manage corrections in monitoring outputs.
