# Land Cover Map 2007 (LCM2007)

- Formats available to suit user requirements: vector data and raster data.
- Vector data:
  - Most detailed product: each polygon represents a land parcel with attributes describing land cover and metadata describing derivation.
  - Minimum mapable unit: 0.5 hectares.
  - Supply format: ESRI Shapefile.
  - Coverage: UK.
- Raster data:
  - Five raster products; a 25 x 25 m raster gives the most likely Broad Habitat per pixel.
  - Four freely available 1 x 1 km rasters summarize the 25 x 25 m raster.
  - Scales: 25 m and 1 km pixel sizes.
  - Supply format: GeoTiff.
  - Coverage: UK.
