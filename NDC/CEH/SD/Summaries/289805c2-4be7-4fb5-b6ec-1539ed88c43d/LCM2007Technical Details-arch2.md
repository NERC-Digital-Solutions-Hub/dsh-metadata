# Land Cover Map 2007

## Overview for environmental analysts

- LCM2007 is available in multiple formats to support diverse monitoring needs across the UK.
- Provides both detailed vector and raster representations of land cover for standardized environmental analysis.

## Vector data

- Most detailed product: each polygon represents a land cover parcel with attributes describing land cover and metadata on how the information was derived.
- Minimum mapable unit: 0.5 hectares.
- Supply format: ESRI Shapefile.
- Coverage: United Kingdom.
- Documentation referenced for further details on attributes and derivation.

## Raster data

- Five raster products in total.
- 25 x 25 meter raster: indicates the most likely Broad Habitat for each pixel.
- Four freely available 1 x 1 kilometer rasters summarise the 25 x 25 meter raster data.
- Scales: 25 m and 1 km pixel size.
- Supply format: GeoTiff.
- Coverage: United Kingdom.
- Detailed metadata for raster formats available in the dataset documentation.
