# Land Cover Map 2007 (LCM2007) is available in different formats, to suit the requirements of various users.

- ## Overview
  - LCM2007 is provided in multiple formats (vector and raster) to meet diverse user needs and applications.

- ## Vector data
  - Most detailed product: polygons representing land cover with attributes and metadata on derivation.
  - Minimum mapable unit: 0.5 hectares.
  - Supply format: ESRI Shapefile.
  - Geographic coverage: United Kingdom (UK).

- ## Raster data
  - Five raster products:
    - A 25 x 25 m raster for the most likely Broad Habitat per pixel.
    - Four freely available 1 x 1 km rasters summarising the 25 x 25 m data.
  - Detailed metadata available in the Land Cover Map 2007 Dataset Documentation.
  - Scales: 25 m and 1 km pixel size.
  - Supply format: GeoTiff.
  - Coverage: UK.

- ## Documentation and metadata
  - Detailed information is available in the Land Cover Map 2007 Dataset Documentation, including:
    - Table 1: Attributes of the LCM2007 vector dataset.
    - Table 2: Habitats, LCM2007 classes and Broad Habitat sub-classes.

- ## Data governance considerations for Data Stewards
  - Ensure formats and metadata meet user needs; maintain clear guidance and tooling.
  - Identify data availability, restrictions (embargoes, proprietary issues), and update mechanisms.
  - Manage data intake from data creators; perform quality assurance, cleaning, and transformation prior to sharing.
  - Respect sharing limitations and maintain systems for dataset updates.
  - Upload and catalog datasets in relevant portals; document work performed on datasets where appropriate.
