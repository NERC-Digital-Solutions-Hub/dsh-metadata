# Details of data structure

## Summary
- The dataset comprises interview transcripts and related documents collected from villages in Lioma, focusing on environmental knowledge and community processes.
- It preserves anonymity of interviewees and uses multiple data collection methods to support triangulation of environmental insights.

## Dataset contents and structure
- Total documents: 34 rtf and 20 csv files (54 documents overall).
- Document types and counts:
  - Participatory_map_engl: 10
  - Participatory_map_port: 10
  - Seasonal_calendar_port: 3
  - Seasonal_calendar_engl: 4
  - Trendanalysis_port: 5
  - Trendanalysis_engl: 10
  - Village_profiles: 1
  - Wealthranking_qual: 11
- Activities covered:
  - Participatory mapping (10 English, 10 Portuguese)
  - Seasonal calendars (3 Portuguese, 4 English)
  - Trend analysis (5 Portuguese, 10 English)
  - Village profiles (Portuguese with English transcripts compiled later)
  - Wealth rankings qualitative data (Portuguese with English transcripts compiled later)

- Field notes in local language; transcripts in Portuguese; English translations audited by a third party to ensure accuracy.

## Geographic scope and village coverage
- Lioma region; a two-week pre-visit in July 2015 covered 13 villages for village leader and representative interviews.
- Four study villages involved in seasonal calendars and trend analysis components.

## Data provenance and quality
- Data compiled from multiple sources and activities to enable cross-checks and environmental interpretations.
- Standardized file formats (rtf and csv) to support uniform analysis and potential data sharing.
