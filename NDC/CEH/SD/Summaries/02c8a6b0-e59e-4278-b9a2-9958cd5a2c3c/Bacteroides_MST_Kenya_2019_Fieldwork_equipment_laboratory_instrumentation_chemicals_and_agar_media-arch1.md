## Agar media
- Intestinal enterococci: Slanetz and Bartley medium (Oxoid CM0377)
- E. coli and Total coliforms: Chromogenic Coliform Agar (Oxoid CM1205B)
- Somatic coliphages: Agar Bacteriological № 1 (OXOID LP 0011) with specific meat peptone, yeast extract, etc.
- Bacteroides phages (BPRM): Agar Bacteriological № 1 with meat peptone, casein peptone/tryptone, yeast extract, NaCl, L-cysteine, D-glucose, MgSO4, CaCl2, haemin, Na2CO3
- General preparation components: Distilled water; Ringers Solution (OXOID BR 00526); Virkon tablets; Hydrochloric acid; Kanamycin monosulfate; Nalidixic acid; NaOH; BSA; Sucrose; Glycerol
