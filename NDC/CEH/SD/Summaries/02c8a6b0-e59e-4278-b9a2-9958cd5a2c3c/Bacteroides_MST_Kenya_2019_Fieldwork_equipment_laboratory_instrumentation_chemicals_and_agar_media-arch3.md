# Fieldwork equipment and Laboratory consumables

## Overview
- An exhaustive inventory of field and laboratory supplies for environmental sampling and microbiological analysis, including field gear, consumables, instrumentation, chemicals, agar media, and general supplies. Some items are specified with brand names and catalogue codes.

## Fieldwork equipment
- 50 ml sterile pots
- Wide Mouth HDPE bottles (500 ml and 1000 ml)
- Alcohol
- Sampling pole and sampling scoop (adapted)
- Sterile wooden spatulas
- Nitrile gloves and masks
- Plastic bags
- Cooler and ice packs
- Red biohazard disposal bags
- Sampling proforma, writing board, pens, and permanent markers

## Laboratory consumables
- pH testing strips
- Microscope slides and slide oil
- Test tube racks and various glass tubes (14 ml – 125 mm)
- Sterilin tubes; centrifuge tubes (50 ml) with screw tops
- Glass reagent/media bottles (100 ml, 250 ml, 500 ml, 1000 ml) with blue pouring rings
- Cryogenic protector tubes and boxes
- Dyes and fixing reagents (e.g., Crystal Violet, Iodine, Saffrine)
- Acetone; spread plate slicks; loops
- Plastic squeeze pipettes; Eppendorf pipettes (100 µl; 1 ml; 5 ml; 10 ml) and tips
- Anaerobic sachets (AnaeroGen) and anaerobic flasks
- Petri dishes (55 mm and 90 mm) with lids
- Media components and select antibiotics/chemicals (e.g., Kanamycin, Nalidixic acid)
- General lab reagents: NaOH, BSA, Sucrose, Glycerol, L-cysteine, MgSO4, MgCl2, CaCl2, NaCl, Glucose, Esch…/bile salts, haemin, and related ingredients
- Specific media formulations for microbial targets:
  - Intestinal enterococci: Slanetz and Bartley medium
  - E. coli and total coliforms: Chromogenic Coliform Agar
  - Somatic coliphages: MSA-based media components and related supplements
  - Bacteroides phages: Agar Bacteriological №1 and supporting components

## Laboratory instrumentation
- Microscope; spectrophotometer
- Distilled water dispenser; water bath
- Vortex; microwave; autoclave (and autoclave tap)
- Gas-fueled Bunsen burner
- 4°C fridge; -20°C freezer
- Colony counter; centrifuge
- Wax crayons; Sharpies; labeling tape
- Ruler; pencils; erasers

## Chemicals
- Agar bacteriological; hemin; various salts and acids/bases:
  - Sodium hydroxide (2 N) solution; calcium chloride dihydrate; sodium carbonate
  - Hydrochloric acid solution; sucrose; BSA; tryptone; yeast extract
  - Sodium chloride; monohydrated L-cysteine; glucose; magnesium salts (MgSO4·7H2O, MgCl2)
  - Magnesium/calcium salts; bile salts; esculine; ammonium ferric citrate
  - Antibiotics/selection agents: gentamicin; kanamycin monosulfate; nalidixic acid
  - Glycerol; additional sucrose and related media components

## Agar media
- General composition and base components for preparations:
  - Distilled water; Ringers solution; Virkon tablets
  - Hydrochloric acid; Kanamycin; Nalidixic acid; NaOH
  - BSA; Sucrose; Glycerol
- Specific media components listed for enteric, coliform, phage, and related microbial targets including peptones, yeast extract, NaCl, L-cysteine, D-glucose, MgSO4, CaCl2, haemin, and related reagents
- Brand/product references for media formulations (e.g., Slanetz and Bartley; Chromogenic Coliform Agar; Agar Bacteriological №1)

## General
- Essential supportive materials and reagents:
  - Distilled water; Ringers solution; Virkon tablets
  - Hydrochloric acid; kanamycin; nalidixic acid; NaOH
  - Bovine serum albumin; Sucrose; Glycerol
