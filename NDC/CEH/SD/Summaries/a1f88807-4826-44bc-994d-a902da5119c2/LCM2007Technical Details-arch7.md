# Land Cover Map 2007 (LCM2007) is available in different formats, to suit the requirements of various users.

- Purpose and scope: Provides UK land cover information in both vector and raster formats to support GIS analyses and map-based visualisations for diverse audiences.
  
## Vector data
- Most detailed product: Each polygon represents a land parcel with attributes describing land cover.
- Metadata: Describes how the information was derived (see Land Cover Map 2007 Dataset Documentation).
- Minimum Mapable Unit: 0.5 hectares.
- Supply Format: ESRI Shapefile.
- Coverage: United Kingdom (UK).

## Raster data
- Total rasters: Five raster products.
- 25 x 25 m raster: Assigns the most likely Broad Habitat for each pixel.
- 1 x 1 km rasters: Four freely available products that summarise the 25 x 25 m data.
- Metadata: Detailed information available in the Dataset Documentation.
- Scales and formats: 25 m and 1 km pixel sizes; Supply Format is GeoTiff.
- Coverage: United Kingdom (UK).
