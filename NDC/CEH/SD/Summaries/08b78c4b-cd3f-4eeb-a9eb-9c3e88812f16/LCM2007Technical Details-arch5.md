# Land Cover Map 2007 (LCM2007) is available in different formats, to suit the requirements of various users.

## Overview
- Provides UK land cover information in both vector and raster formats designed for diverse user needs.
- Detailed data and accompanying documentation exist to support interpretation and use.

## Data Formats and Coverage

### Vector data
- Most detailed product: each polygon represents a land cover parcel with attributes and metadata about derivation.
- Minimum Mapable Unit for land cover parcels: 0.5 ha.
- Supply format: ESRI Shapefile.
- Coverage: United Kingdom (UK).

### Raster data
- Five raster products available to summarize information.
- 25 x 25 m raster: indicates the most likely Broad Habitat for each pixel.
- Four freely available 1 x 1 km raster products: summarize the 25 x 25 m raster data.
- Scales: 25 m and 1 km pixel sizes.
- Supply format: GeoTiff.
- Coverage: UK.
