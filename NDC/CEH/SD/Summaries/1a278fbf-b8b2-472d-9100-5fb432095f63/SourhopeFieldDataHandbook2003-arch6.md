## Soil Profile Description (Block 4)
- Horizons and depths:
  - LF: 0–1 cm — fibrous, moist, no mineral grains.
  - FH: 1–4 cm — fibrous, moist, abundant very fine fibrous roots.
  - H: 4–8 cm — black matrix colour, amorphous, moist, weak blocky structure; roots present.
  - Ah: 8–29 cm — dark reddish brown, sandy silt loam; friable; abundant roots; many igneous stones (andesite).
  - Abg: 29–40 cm — pinkish grey with mottling; coarse sandy silt loam; moderate blocky structure; many igneous stones.
  - B sg: 40–61 cm — brown; fine mottles; moist; firm; many very fine roots; many igneous stones.
  - C gx: 61–95 cm — reddish brown to brown; sandy clay loam; massive to platy structure; moist; igneous stones present.
- Key features: fibrous surface layers; increasing weathering with depth; abundant igneous stones (andesite) especially in Ah and Abg; acidic conditions expected in surface horizons with progressive changes deeper down.
