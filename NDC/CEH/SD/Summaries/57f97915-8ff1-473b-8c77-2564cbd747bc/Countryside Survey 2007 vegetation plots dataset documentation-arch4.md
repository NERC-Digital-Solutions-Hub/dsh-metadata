# Countryside Survey 2007: vegetation plot data, Great Britain

## Overview
- A two-file dataset from Countryside Survey 2007 detailing vegetation plots across Great Britain.
- Files: one with plot information, one with species lists; data documented in Maskell et al., 2008.
- Published and maintained by NERC - Centre for Ecology & Hydrology; acknowledged in all copies and publications.

## Dataset contents and structure
- Vegetation Plot - Plot Information 2007.csv
  - Columns: YEAR, SQUARE_ID, PLOT_ID, PLOT_TYPE, COUNTRY, ENV_ZONE_2007, EZ_DESC_07
- Vegetation Plot - Species List 2007.csv
  - Columns: YEAR, SQUARE_ID, PLOT_ID, AMALG_PTYPE, BRC_NUMBER, BRC_NAMES, NEST_LEVEL, ZERO_COVER, FIRST_COVER, TOTAL_COVER
- Species nomenclature follows Stace, 1997 (New Flora of the British Isles).
- BRC_NUMBER serves as the species identifier; BRC_NAMES provides the species name.
- Coverage metrics are provided as ZERO_COVER, FIRST_COVER, and TOTAL_COVER for each plot.

## Data provenance and standards
- Year of survey: 2007; England, Scotland, and Wales included.
- ENV_ZONE_2007 and EZ_DESC_07 describe environmental zones used for spatial contextualization.
- Data structure aligns with Countryside Survey methodologies; consult Maskell et al., 2008 for further details and the Vegetation Plots Handbook.
