- Sampling and timing: changes to sampling interval affect temporal resolution and comparability.
- Metadata and documentation: scattered notes (offsets, maintenance, degassing, grass cutting) highlight the need for comprehensive metadata and change logs.
- Data integrity risks: irregular blips and unexplained data gaps necessitate validation, anomaly detection, and clear reporting of issues.
