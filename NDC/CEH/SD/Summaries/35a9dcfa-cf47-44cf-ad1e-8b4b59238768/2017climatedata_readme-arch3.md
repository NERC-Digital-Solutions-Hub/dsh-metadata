# Data collected 2017-18 during field transplant on Mt Etna

- Overview
  - At each transplant elevation on Mt Etna, three Tinytag dataloggers were deployed at plant height adjacent to the transplanted plants.
  - Dataloggers recorded for 1 year; daily minimum and maximum temperatures (Temp.Min and Temp.Max) were extracted in degrees Celsius.

- Quality control
  - Dataloggers were kept together before deployment to ensure accuracy.

- Variables
  - Elevation: 500 m, 1000 m, 1500 m, 2000 m ASL
  - Block: A, B, C (replicates within elevation)
  - Date: date of temperature recording
  - Temp.Min: daily minimum temperature (°C)
  - Temp.Max: daily maximum temperature (°C)
