# UrineComposition.csv

- Project: Uplands-N2O
- Grant number: NE/M015351/1
- Authors: Karina A Marsden, Lucy Lush, Jon A Holmberg, Mick J Whelan, Andrew J King, Rory P Wilson, Alice F Charteris, Laura M Cardenas, Davey L Jones, Dave R Chadwick
- Summary: Dataset of Welsh Mountain ewe urine chemical composition and associated metadata, produced via laboratory analysis and standardized sample processing.

## Data description

- Purpose: Characterise urine chemistry and dissolved organic content to support understanding of nitrogen and carbon flows in upland environments.
- Sample type: Individual ewe urine events from Welsh Mountain sheep.
- Sample handling: Filtered on ice through Whatman No.1 filter papers (11 μm) prior to freezing; stored at -20 °C before analysis.
- Key measurements: pH, electrical conductivity (EC), total nitrogen (TN), dissolved organic carbon (TOC), urea, nitrate, ammonium, free amino acids (AA), allantoin, creatinine, uric acid, hippuric acid, benzoic acid, calcium, sodium, potassium.
- Analytical platforms: 
  - TOC and TN – Multi N/C 2100S analyser (AnalytikJena)
  - Urea – enzymatic method (Orsenneau et al., 1992)
  - Nitrate – colorimetric (Mulvaney, 1996)
  - Ammonium – colorimetric (Miranda et al., 2001)
  - Free amino acids – fluorometric (Jones et al., 2002)
  - Allantoin, creatinine, uric acid, hippuric acid, benzoic acid – HPLC (Varian Pro Star 310, C18, 5 μm, 250 × 4.6 mm)
  - Detection: UV at 218 nm; flow 1 mL min-1; mobile phase A (KH2PO4, pH 4) and B (60% A, 40% methanol)
  - Amino acids, allantoin, creatinine, uric acid, hippuric acid, benzoic acid, Ca, Na, K – measured with specified methods
  - Calibrations and dilutions applied as necessary

## Data headers (variables)

- Season: Spring, Summer, or Autumn (season of urine collection)
- Site: Semi-improved or improved pasture
- Sheep: Replicate sheep identifier
- Date: Collection date (dd/mm/yyyy)
- Time: Approximate urination time (hh:mm)
- Vol: Urine event volume (ml)
- TOC: Urine dissolved organic carbon (g C l-1)
- TN: Urine total nitrogen (g N l-1)
- pH: Urine pH
- EC: Urine electrical conductivity (mS cm-1)
- Urea: Urea content (g urea-N l-1)
- Nitrate: Nitrate content (mg NO3--N l-1)
- Ammonium: Ammonium content (mg NH4+-N l-1)
- AA: Amino acids content (mg amino acid-N l-1)
- Allantoin: Allantoin content (g compound l-1)
- Creatinine: Creatinine content (mg compound l-1)
- Uric: Uric acid content (mg compound l-1)
- Hippuric: Hippuric acid content (g compound l-1)
- Benzoic: Benzoic acid content (mg compound l-1)
- Ca: Calcium content (mg Ca l-1)
- Na: Sodium content (mg Na l-1)
- K: Potassium content (g K l-1)
