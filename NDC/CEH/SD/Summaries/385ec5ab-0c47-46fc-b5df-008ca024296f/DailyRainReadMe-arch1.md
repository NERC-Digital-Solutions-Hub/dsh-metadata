# Dailyrain.csv

## Overview
- Daily rainfall data for four urine collection trials with Welsh Mountain ewes conducted in 2016.

## Trials and site context
- Four trials/sites: spring semi-improved, summer semi-improved, autumn semi-improved, and autumn improved pasture.
- Weather data collected at the sites to accompany the trials.

## Data source and method
- Weather data from a meteorological station installed at the sites (Skye Instrument, Llandrindod Wells, UK).
- Measurement instrument: tipping bucket rain gauge.

## Data structure (variables)
- Site: Semi-Improved or Improved pasture.
- Season: spring, summer, or autumn (for semi-improved) or autumn (for the improved site).
- Date: measurement date in dd/mm/yyyy.
- Rainfall: daily rainfall totals in millimetres (mm).

## Temporal and spatial coverage
- Year: 2016.
- Locations correspond to four pasture-site combinations used in the urine collection trials.
