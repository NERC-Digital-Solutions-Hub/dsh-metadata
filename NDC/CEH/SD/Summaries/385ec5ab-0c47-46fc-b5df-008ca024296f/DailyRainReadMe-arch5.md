# Dailyrain.csv

## Overview
- Daily rainfall data for four urine collection trials with Welsh Mountain ewes, conducted in 2016 across four site/season configurations: spring semi-improved, summer semi-improved, autumn semi-improved, and autumn improved.
- Weather data collected from a meteorological station at the sites (Skye Instrument, Llandrindod Wells, UK) using a tipping bucket rain gauge.

## Data provenance and metadata
- Project: Uplands-N2O
- Grant number: NE/M015351/1
- Authors: Karina A Marsden, Lucy Lush, Jon A Holmberg, Mick J Whelan, Andrew J King, Rory P Wilson, Alice F Charteris, Laura M Cardenas, Davey L Jones, Dave R Chadwick
- Data reuse: Please ensure data are fully cited with appropriate attribution.

## Data structure and fields
- Headers/variables:
  - Site: Semi-Improved or Improved pasture
  - Season: Spring, Summer, or Autumn (for semi-improved) or Autumn (for improved)
  - Date: Date of measurement in dd/mm/yyyy
  - Rainfall: Daily rainfall totals in millimetres (mm)

## Data quality and stewardship considerations
- Ensure consistent categorization of Site and Season across records.
- Validate Date formatting (dd/mm/yyyy) and handle any missing values.
- Confirm Rainfall units are consistently in millimetres.
- Record any data gaps, sensor outages, or calibration notes from the tipping bucket rain gauge.
