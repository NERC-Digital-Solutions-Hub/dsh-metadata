# Dailyrain.csv

## Overview
- Dataset of daily rainfall data for four urine collection trials with Welsh Mountain ewes, conducted in 2016.
- Project: Uplands-N2O, Grant NE/M015351/1.
- Authors: Karina A Marsden, Lucy Lush, Jon A Holmberg, Mick J Whelan, Andrew J King, Rory P Wilson, Alice F Charteris, Laura M Cardenas, Davey L Jones, Dave R Chadwick.
- Data reuse should include full citation.

## Data Source and Collection
- Weather data sourced from a meteorological station at the sites (Skye Instrument, Llandrindod Wells, UK) with a tipping bucket rain gauge.
- Four trials/sites/seasons:
  - 1) Spring semi-improved site
  - 2) Summer semi-improved site
  - 3) Autumn semi-improved site
  - 4) Autumn improved site

## Data Structure and Headers
- Site: Semi-Improved or Improved pasture
- Season: Spring, Summer, or Autumn for semi-improved; Autumn for the improved site
- Date: Measurement date in dd/mm/yyyy
- Rainfall: Daily rainfall totals in mm

## Temporal and Spatial Coverage
- Timeframe: 2016
- Spatial scope: Four pasture sites with corresponding seasons

## Data Provenance and Governance
- Original data originators listed with project affiliation.
- Data reuse requires full citation; provenance tied to specific weather station measurements.
- Metadata and data presentation are designed for clarity, including clear variable definitions and date formats.
