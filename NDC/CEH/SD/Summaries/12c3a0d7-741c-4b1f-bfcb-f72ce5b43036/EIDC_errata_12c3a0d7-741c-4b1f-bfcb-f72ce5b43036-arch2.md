# Errata for dataset https://catalogue.ceh.ac.uk/documents/12c3a0d7-741c-4b1f-bfcb-f72ce5b43036

## Overview
- Erratum notice for a CEH dataset.
- NoData values were incorrect in the SPI18 and SPI24 data files.

## Impact on environmental monitoring
- Affects data quality and missing-data handling forSPI18/SPI24 outputs.
- May influence standardized environmental health indicators, maps, and reports that rely on accurate NoData values.

## Recommended actions for analysts and data managers
- Retrieve and replace with corrected SPI18 and SPI24 data files.
- Re-run quality assurance, data cleaning, and transformations as needed.
- Update analyses, maps, and reports to reflect corrected NoData values.
- Ensure corrected datasets are uploaded to the appropriate data portals and that metadata notes the erratum.

## Data management and dissemination
- Document the correction and version the dataset to reflect the change.
- Inform users of the updated data to prevent misinterpretation of prior NoData values.
- Maintain clear provenance linking to the erratum for transparency.
