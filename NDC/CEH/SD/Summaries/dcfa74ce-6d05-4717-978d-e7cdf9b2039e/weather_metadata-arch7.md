# 2018 weather station data from Durleigh Reservoir metadata file.

## Overview
- Weather station: Delta T WS-GP1; purpose to determine on-site weather conditions through 2018 to support measurements of in-reservoir processes.
- Authors: Slavin and Wain (2018). Dataset cited as: Slavin, E.I., Wain, D.J. 2018. Weather data from Durleigh Reservoir, 2018.
- Measurements span: 05/04/2018 to 05/10/2018.
- Location context: attached to the side of a building, 4 m above the reservoir surface.

## Spatial and Temporal Extents
- Coordinates: latitude 51.1195, longitude -3.0389.
- Time format: dd/mm/yyyy HH:MM.
- Temporal coverage: 2018 data collection period specified above.

## Data Collection and Access
- Deployment: Weather station deployed by Emily Slavin and Danielle Wain.
- Purpose of data: support measurements of in-reservoir processes by providing local weather context.
- Data resolution: measurements are averaged over 10-minute intervals.

## Parameters and Sensors
- Air temperature (°C): sensor RHT3nl-CA; range 20–70; accuracy ±0.3; 10-minute averages.
- Humidity (%RH): sensor RHT3nl-CA; range -5% to 95% RH at 23°C; accuracy ±2%; 10-minute averages.
- Radiation (W/m^2): sensor D-PYRPA-CA; range 0–1.1 kW/m^2; accuracy ±5%; 10-minute averages.
- Rainfall (mm): sensor RG2+WS-CA; funnel diameter 160 mm; accuracy 0.2 mm per tip; 10-minute intervals.
- Wind Direction (degrees): sensor D-034B-CA; range 0–360°; accuracy ±4°; 10-minute averages.
- Wind Speed (m/s): sensor D-034B-CA; range 0.4–75 m/s; accuracy ±0.1 m/s; 10-minute averages.

## Data Quality and Notes
- Data presented as 10-minute averaged measurements to balance resolution and manageability for GIS-based analyses.
- Location and sensor details facilitate spatial integration with reservoir-site analyses and map-based visualizations.
