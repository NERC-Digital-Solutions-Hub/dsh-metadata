# 2018 weather station data from Durleigh Reservoir metadata file

## Overview
- Metadata and sensor specifications for the Delta T WS-GP1 weather station at Durleigh Reservoir.
- Purpose: determine on-site weather conditions through 2018 to support measurements of in-reservoir processes.

## Location and deployment
- Location: latitude 51.1195, longitude -3.0389
- Installation: attached to the side of a building, positioned 4 m above the reservoir surface

## Data period
- Measurements collected between 05/04/2018 and 05/10/2018
- Most sensors provide data averaged over 10-minute intervals

## Authors and citation
- Authors: Slavin and Wain (2018)
- Dataset citation: Slavin, E.I., Wain, D.J. 2018. Weather data from Durleigh Reservoir, 2018.

## Sensor specifications and measurements
- Air temperature (°C): RHT3nl-CA; range 20–70; accuracy ±0.3; 10-minute averages
- Humidity (%RH): RHT3nl-CA; range -5% to 95% at 23°C; accuracy ±2%; 10-minute averages
- Radiation (W/m²): D-PYRPA-CA; range 0–1.1 kW/m²; accuracy ±5%; 10-minute averages
- Rainfall (mm): RG2+WS-CA; funnel diameter 160 mm; accuracy 0.2 mm per tip; 10-minute intervals
- Wind direction (degrees): D-034B-CA; range 0–360°; accuracy ±4°; 10-minute averages
- Wind speed (m/s): D-034B-CA; range 0.4–75 m/s; accuracy ±0.1 m/s; 10-minute averages

## Data quality and handling
- Data are collected and summarized at 10-minute intervals, with specified sensor ranges and accuracies to support reliable interpretation of on-site weather conditions.

## Purpose and usage
- On-site weather conditions documented to support measurements of in-reservoir processes during 2018.
- Station deployed and maintained by Emily Slavin and Danielle Wain.
