# 2018 weather station data from Durleigh Reservoir metadata file

## Overview
- Metadata file for weather station Delta T WS-GP1 at Durleigh Reservoir.
- Purpose: determine on-site weather conditions in 2018 to support measurements of in-reservoir processes.
- Deployment: attached to the side of a building, located 4 m above the reservoir surface.
- Time format for timestamps: dd/mm/yyyy HH:MM.
- Measurement period: 05/04/2018 to 05/10/2018.
- Authors: Slavin and Wain (2018). Dataset should be cited as: Slavin, E.I., Wain, D.J. 2018. Weather data from Durleigh Reservoir, 2018.

## Location & deployment
- Latitude: 51.1195
- Longitude: -3.0389
- Sensor mounted on building exterior, 4 m above reservoir surface.

## Data collection details
- Purpose: support measurements of in-reservoir processes through on-site weather conditions.
- Measurements are aggregated at 10-minute intervals.

## Sensors & measurements
- Air temperature (°C): RHT3nl-CA
  - Range: 20 to 70
  - Accuracy: ±0.3
  - Sampling: 10-minute averages
- Humidity (%): RHT3nl-CA
  - Range: -5% to 95% RH at 23°C
  - Accuracy: ±2%
  - Sampling: 10-minute averages
- Radiation (W/m^2): D-PYRPA-CA
  - Range: 0 to 1.1 kW m-2
  - Accuracy: ±5%
  - Sampling: 10-minute averages
- Rainfall (mm): RG2+WS-CA
  - Range: 160 mm funnel diameter
  - Accuracy: 0.2 mm per tip
  - Sampling: 10-minute intervals
- Wind Direction (degrees): D-034B-CA
  - Range: 0 to 360
  - Accuracy: ±4°
  - Sampling: 10-minute averages
- Wind Speed (m/s): D-034B-CA
  - Range: 0.4 to 75
  - Accuracy: ±0.1
  - Sampling: 10-minute averages
