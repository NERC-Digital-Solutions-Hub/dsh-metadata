# 2018 weather station data from Durleigh Reservoir metadata file.

- Purpose and scope
  - Weather data collected to determine on-site weather conditions to support measurements of in-reservoir processes.

- Location and timeframe
  - Weather station: Delta T WS-GP1
  - Location: attached to the side of a building, positioned 4 m above the reservoir surface
  - Coordinates: lat 51.1195, lon -3.0389
  - Measurement period: 05/04/2018 to 05/10/2018
  - Sampling: 10-minute intervals (averaged)

- Authors and citation
  - Authors: Slavin and Wain (2018)
  - Dataset citation: Slavin, E.I., Wain, D.J. 2018. Weather data from Durleigh Reservoir, 2018.
  - Data collection credited to Emily Slavin and Danielle Wain

- Measured parameters and sensors
  - Air temperature (°C)
    - Sensor: RHT3nl-CA combined air temperature sensor
    - Range: 20 to 70 °C
    - Accuracy: ± 0.3 °C
  - Humidity (%)
    - Sensor: RHT3nl-CA
    - Range: -5% to 95% relative humidity at 23 °C
    - Accuracy: ± 2% RH
  - Radiation (W/m²)
    - Sensor: D-PYRPA-CA
    - Range: 0 to 1.1 kW m⁻²
    - Accuracy: ± 5%
  - Rainfall (mm)
    - Sensor: RG2+WS-CA
    - Range: 160 mm funnel diameter
    - Accuracy: 0.2 mm per tip
  - Wind direction (degrees)
    - Sensor: D-034B-CA
    - Range: 0 to 360°
    - Accuracy: ± 4°
  - Wind speed (m/s)
    - Sensor: D-034B-CA
    - Range: 0.4 to 75 m s⁻¹
    - Accuracy: ± 0.1 m s⁻¹

- Data quality and usage notes
  - Measurements are intended to document on-site meteorological conditions to support reservoir process studies.
  - All parameters include explicit sensor types, ranges, and accuracy to assess data quality for analysis and modeling.
