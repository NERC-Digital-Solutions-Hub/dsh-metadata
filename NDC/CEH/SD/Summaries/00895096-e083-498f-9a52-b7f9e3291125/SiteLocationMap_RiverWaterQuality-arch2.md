# PANG LAMBOURN CATCHMENT: RIVER WATER QUALITY SITE (LCWQ)

## Overview
- Documents a river water quality monitoring site within the Pang Lambourn Catchment, identified as LCWQ.
- Aims to support consistent assessment of environmental health and policy performance over time through standardized data.

## Data and Quality Assurance
- Data collected from the monitoring site are understood, verified, and quality assured.
- Data are cleaned and transformed using standardized methods to produce usable outputs.

## Analysis and Outputs
- Data are analyzed with standardised approaches to produce outputs such as indicators of river water quality.
- Outputs are presented in clear formats (e.g., reports, maps, charts).

## Data Management and Access
- Created datasets are stored appropriately and uploaded to relevant portals.
- Efforts are made to enable access to the underlying data used to generate final outputs.

## Challenges and Opportunities
- Increasing the value of monitoring datasets by combining them with other relevant data (avoiding single-use data).
- Enhancing accessibility of underlying data to broader audiences for scrutiny and reuse.
