# Physical and nutrient monitoring location for River Enborne, Berkshire

- Purpose and coverage
  - Geospatial dataset identifying the physical and nutrient monitoring location along the River Enborne near Newbury, Berkshire.
  - Map includes surrounding towns (Thatcham, Kingsclere, Tadley) and the River Enborne, with a distance scale.

- Data content
  - Geographical coordinates and site location for monitoring activities.
  - Visual map representation to aid discovery and use.

- Metadata, quality, and standards
  - Requires clear metadata (data source, date, coordinate reference system) and consistent naming.
  - Quality assurance steps needed: verify coordinates, ensure alignment with current monitoring sites, standardize formats.

- Access, sharing, and publication
  - Dataset should be uploaded to relevant data portals and catalogued for discoverability.
  - Document any data processing or transformations applied to the dataset.

- User needs and usability
  - Supports researchers, policymakers, and site managers in locating and utilizing monitoring data.
  - Clear linkage between location details and monitoring activities/timeframes.

- Governance considerations and potential challenges
  - Risk of incomplete user needs or priorities affecting scope.
  - Timeliness of data delivery and updates to reflect changes in sites or measurements.
  - Interoperability across systems and formats, especially for larger or multi-site datasets.
  - Handling outdated baselines or bespoke solutions that hinder sharing and reuse.
