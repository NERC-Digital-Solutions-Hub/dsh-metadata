# ReadMe file for WeatherSoilMoistureData.csv

- Purpose: Provide weather and soil moisture data from the experimental study site for environmental monitoring and analysis, covering seasonal sheep urine application studies.
- Variables included:
  - Rain_mm: rainfall in millimetres
  - SoilTemp_5cm: soil temperature in 째C at 5 cm depth
  - AirTemp: air temperature in 째C
  - WFPS_5: soil water-filled pore space (%-volume) at 5 cm depth
  - WFPS_10: soil water-filled pore space (%-volume) at 10 cm depth
  - TimeDate: date and time of measurements (format dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm:ss)

## Data collection and sources

- Meteorological data collected from a station installed at the site by Skye Instruments Ltd. (Llandrindod Wells, UK).
- Soil moisture (WFPS) calculated from 10HS Moisture Sensor probes (Decagon Devices Inc., USA) and calibrated according to manufacturer instructions.
