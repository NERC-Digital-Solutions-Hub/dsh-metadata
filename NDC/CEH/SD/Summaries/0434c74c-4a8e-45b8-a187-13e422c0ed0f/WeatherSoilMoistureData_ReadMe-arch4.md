# ReadMe file for WeatherSoilMoistureData.csv

## Overview
- Data record rainfall, soil temperature at 5 cm depth, air temperature, and soil water-filled pore space (WFPS) at 5 cm and 10 cm depth.
- Collected at the Henfaes Research Station, Abergwyngregyn, North Wales (270 m a.s.l.; 53째13'N, 4째0'W).
- Time period covers seasonal sheep urine application studies.

## Data provenance and collection
- Weather data gathered from a meteorological station installed at the experimental site (Skye Instruments Ltd., Llandrindod Wells, UK).
- Soil WFPS values derived from soil moisture probes (10HS Moisture Sensor, Decagon Devices Inc., WA, USA).
- Probes calibrated according to the manufacturers' instructions.
