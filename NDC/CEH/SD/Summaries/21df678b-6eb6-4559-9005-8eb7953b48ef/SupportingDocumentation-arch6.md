# Groundwater level information for 10 locations within the Gandak River Basin, in Bihar, India

## Overview
- Data collection as part of the CHANSE (Coupled Human And Natural Systems Environment) project.
- Recording period: April 2017 to February 2019.
- Instrumentation: Troll automatic groundwater level loggers with recordings at 15-minute intervals.
- Data corrections: All measurements corrected to local barometric pressure using Troll barometer.

## Data and methods
- Data include groundwater level measurements and associated variables collected at 15-minute intervals.
- Data corrected for barometric pressure to ensure comparability.
- Data are organized by site with metadata on installation and data collection periods.

## Variable descriptions
- DateTime: Date and time of recording.
- Temp: Water temperature.
- mbgl: Depth to water level below ground level (logger measurement).
- man_mbgl: Manually measured depth to groundwater level (dip meter).
- PT: Pressure transducer.
- Baro: Barometer reading.
- Missing values: Represented as blanks.
