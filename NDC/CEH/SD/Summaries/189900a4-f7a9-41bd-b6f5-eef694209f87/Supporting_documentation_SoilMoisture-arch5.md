# Soil moisture profiles from permafrost in subarctic Canada

## Overview
- Dataset documenting soil moisture profiles measured during summers 2013 and 2014, alongside related gas flux datasets.
- Measurements taken at multiple sites in subarctic Canada, using a standardized protocol to obtain volumetric soil moisture (m3 m-3) at multiple depths.
- Site codes and forest/peatland descriptions provided to contextualize the data (facilitating future discovery and reuse).

## Dataset details
- Timeframe: Summers 2013 and 2014.
- Measurements: volumetric soil moisture (m3 m-3) at four depths: 10, 20, 30, and 40 cm.
- Equipment: PR2 profile probe with 4 sensing elements and a portable reading meter HH2 (Delta-T Devices Ltd, Cambridge, UK); access tubes inserted in soil using an augering kit to minimize disruption.
- Protocol highlights:
  - Access tubes placed as deep as thaw depth allowed, then deeper as the active layer thickened.
  - PR2 profile probe connected to the HH2 meter, removed from protective tube, and inserted into access tubes during measurement.
  - Values recorded from the meter using the default calibration.
- Associated data: mentions gas flux data sets (referenced as part of the broader data collection).

## Site codes and descriptions
- FFU - FireFox Unburnt: An unburnt black spruce forest
- FFB - FireFox Burnt: A black spruce forest burnt in 1998
- BLF - BlackFox: A black spruce forest
- WHF - WhiteFox: A white spruce forest
- TPP - Teslin Peatland Plateau: A peatland plateau near Teslin
- MCU - Mosquito Creek Unburnt: An unburnt black spruce forest
- MCB - Mosquito Creek Burnt: A burnt black spruce forest
- WTP - White Truck Plateau: A peatland plateau near Yellowknife
- BCB - Boundary Creek Birch: A birch forest
- BCS - Boundary Creek Spruce: A black spruce forest
