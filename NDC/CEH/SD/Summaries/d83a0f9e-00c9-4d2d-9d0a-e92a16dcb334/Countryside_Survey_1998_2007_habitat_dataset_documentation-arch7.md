# Dataset Documentation for 'Countryside Survey mapped estimates of Broad Habitat area change in Great Britain between 1998 and 2007'

## Overview
- Provides national estimates of Broad Habitat area change (percent increase/decrease) for Great Britain between 1998 and 2007.
- Calculated using the 2007 ITE Land Classification and summarized by Land Class.
- Mapping methodology described in Maskell et al. (2008) and Barr (1998).

## Data Content
- File: CS98_07_Broad_Habitat_Change_Map.shp (vector shapefile)
- Purpose: show percentage change for each Broad Habitat (Habitats 1–17) across Great Britain.
- Basis: 2007 ITE Land Classification; results structured by Land Class.
- References for methodology: Maskell et al., 2008; Barr, 1998.

## Fields and Attributes
- LC2007: numeric; ITE Land Class (Bunce et al., 1996; Bunce et al., 1981, 2007)
- LC2007_COD: text; ITE Land Class code
- SURVEY: text; Years of survey (to/from)
- Column1: numeric; Broadleaved, Mixed and Yew Woodland — %change
- Column2: numeric; Coniferous Woodland — %change
- Column3: numeric; Boundary and Linear Features — %change
- Column4: numeric; Arable and Horticulture — %change
- Column5: numeric; Improved Grassland — %change
- Column6: numeric; Neutral Grassland — %change
- Column7: numeric; Calcareous Grassland — %change
- Column8: numeric; Acid Grassland — %change
- Column9: numeric; Bracken — %change
- Column10: numeric; Dwarf Shrub Heath — %change
- Column11: numeric; Fen, Marsh, Swamp — %change
- Column12: numeric; Bog — %change
- Column13: numeric; Standing Open Waters and Canals — %change
- Column14: numeric; Rivers and Streams — %change
- Column15: numeric; Montane — %change
- Column16: numeric; Inland Rock — %change
- Column17: numeric; Urban — %change
