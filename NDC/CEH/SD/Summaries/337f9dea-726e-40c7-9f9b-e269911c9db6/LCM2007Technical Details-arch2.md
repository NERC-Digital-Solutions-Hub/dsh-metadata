# Land Cover Map 2007 (LCM2007) is available in different formats, to suit the requirements of various users.

## Overview
- LCM2007 is provided in multiple formats to meet different user needs for land cover information across the UK.
- Supports monitoring and analysis for environmental health and policy performance through standardized data formats and available metadata.

## Data formats

### Vector data
- Most detailed product: each polygon represents a land parcel with land cover attributes and metadata on data derivation.
- Minimum Mapable Unit: 0.5ha.
- Supply format: ESRI Shapefile.
- Coverage: United Kingdom.

### Raster data
- Five raster products in total.
- 25 x 25 m raster: provides the most likely Broad Habitat for each pixel.
- Four freely available 1 x 1 km raster products: summarize the 25 x 25 m raster.
- Scales: 25 m and 1 km pixel sizes.
- Supply format: GeoTiff.
- Coverage: United Kingdom.
