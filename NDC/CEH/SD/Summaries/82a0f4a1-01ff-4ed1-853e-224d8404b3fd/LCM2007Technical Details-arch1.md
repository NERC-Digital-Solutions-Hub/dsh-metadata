# Land Cover Map 2007 (LCM2007) is available in different formats, to suit the requirements of various users.

## Overview
- Land Cover Map 2007 (LCM2007) provides UK land cover data in two main formats: vector and raster.

## Vector data
- Most detailed product: parcels/polygons with land cover attributes and metadata on data derivation.
- Minimum mapable unit: 0.5 hectares.
- Supply format: ESRI Shapefile.
- Coverage: United Kingdom.
- See Land Cover Map 2007 Dataset Documentation for attribute and derivation details.

## Raster data
- Five raster products available.
- 25 x 25 m raster: assigns the most likely Broad Habitat per pixel.
- Four freely available 1 x 1 km rasters: summarise the 25 m raster.
- Scales: 25 m and 1 km pixel sizes.
- Supply format: GeoTiff.
- Coverage: United Kingdom.
- Detailed metadata available in the Land Cover Map 2007 Dataset Documentation.
