# Land Cover Map 2007 (LCM2007) is available in different formats, to suit the requirements of various users.

## Overview
- LCM2007 provides land cover information in multiple formats to meet diverse user needs.
- Primary data products cover the UK.

## Data Formats and Coverage

### Vector data
- Most detailed product: each polygon represents a land parcel with land cover attributes and metadata on derivation.
- Minimum Mapable Unit for land cover parcels: 0.5 ha.
- Supply format: ESRI Shapefile.
- Coverage: UK.

### Raster data
- Five raster products in total.
- A 25 x 25 m raster gives the most likely Broad Habitat for each pixel.
- Four freely available 1 x 1 km rasters summarize the 25 x 25 m raster.
- Detailed metadata available in the Land Cover Map 2007 Dataset Documentation.
- Scales: 25 m and 1 km pixel sizes.
- Supply format: GeoTiff.
- Coverage: UK.
