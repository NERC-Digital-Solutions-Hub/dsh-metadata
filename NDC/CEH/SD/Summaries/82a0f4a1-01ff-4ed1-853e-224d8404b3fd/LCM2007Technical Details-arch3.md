# Land Cover Map 2007 (LCM2007)

- Overview
  - LCM2007 is available in multiple formats to suit different user requirements, supporting environmental monitoring and policy evaluation with detailed land cover information.

- Data formats and coverage
  - Vector data
    - Most detailed product: polygons representing land parcels with land cover attributes and metadata describing how the information was derived.
    - Minimum Mapable Unit: 0.5 hectares.
    - Supply format: ESRI Shapefile.
    - Coverage: UK.
  - Raster data
    - Five raster products: a 25 x 25 m raster giving the most likely Broad Habitat for each pixel, plus four freely available 1 x 1 km rasters that summarize the 25 x 25 m data.
    - Detailed metadata for raster formats available in the dataset documentation.
    - Scales: 25 m and 1 km pixel sizes.
    - Supply format: GeoTiff.
    - Coverage: UK.
