# Supporting documentation for Sample_list.csv

## Overview
- Defines the schema for a environmental monitoring sample list to ensure traceability, consistency, and comparability over time.
- Aligns data capture with standardized field meanings and units, facilitating integration across datasets and monitoring portals.
- Supports structured recording of sample metadata from collection through to analysis context.

## Field definitions

- Sample_Code
  - Explanation: Unique sample identifier.
  - Units: n/a
  - Notes: 

- RAP
  - Explanation: International Commission on Radiological Protection (ICRP) Reference Animals and Plant (RAP) into which the sampled species can be attributed.
  - Units: n/a
  - Notes: 

- Sample_Description
  - Explanation: Part of the organism analysed (e.g., where whole organism was divided and analysed separately).
  - Units: n/a
  - Notes: 

- Collection_Date
  - Explanation: Date of sample collection.
  - Units: dd-mmm-yy
  - Notes: 

- Dry/Fresh_Ratio
  - Explanation: Dry to fresh weight ratio.
  - Units: unitless
  - Notes: 

- Coordinates_of_the_Sample
  - Explanation: Grid reference of sampling location.
  - Units: decimal degrees and minutes
  - Notes: 

- Sample_Association
  - Explanation: Unique sample identifier of soil which was sampled at the same site.
  - Units: n/a
  - Notes: 

- Notes
  - Explanation: Additional comments (where applicable).
  - Units: n/a
