# WildCrickets06F_EarlyCallingVsSurvival Description of content

- Description: A dataset of individual male crickets with lifespan classified by early calling effort as High (H) or Low (L) relative to the population median. 
- Key variables:
  - Year: Year when the cricket was alive.
  - Lifespan: Lifespan in days.
  - EarlyCalling: High (H) or Low (L) effort on early calling.
- Classification logic: EarlyCalling is determined by whether early calling activity exceeds or falls below the population median.
