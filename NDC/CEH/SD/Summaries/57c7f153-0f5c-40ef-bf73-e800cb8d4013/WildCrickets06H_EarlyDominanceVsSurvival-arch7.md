# WildCrickets06H_EarlyDominanceVsSurvival Description of content

## Overview
- Describes individual male crickets categorized by early-life social dominance (high or low) and analyzed in relation to their survival.
- Classification is based on whether early-life dominance is above or below the population median.

## Data fields
- Year: Year when the cricket was alive.
- Lifespan: Lifespan in days.
- EarlyDominance: H (High) or L (Low) dominance in early life.
