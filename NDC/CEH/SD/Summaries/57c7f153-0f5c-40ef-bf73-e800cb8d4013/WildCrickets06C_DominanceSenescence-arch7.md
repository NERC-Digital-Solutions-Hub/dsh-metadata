# WildCrickets06C_DominanceSenescence

## Overview
- Dataset captures success per fight for individual male crickets.
- Records are per fight and include temporal and comparative attributes.

## Key fields
- Year: year when the cricket was alive.
- ID: individual identification.
- Age: age at fight in days.
- AgeDiff: age difference between the cricket and its opponent in days.
- SizeDiff: size difference between the cricket and its opponent in grams.
- Won: fight outcome (1 = won, 0 = did not win).
