# WildCrickets06J_EarlyEmergenceVsSenescence Description of content

- dataset describes singing activity per sample and per individual male, with a classification of early (E) or late (L) emergence relative to the population median in the season.
- aims to capture timing of emergence and related singing behavior to support ecological insights.

## Variables and data model

- Emergence: E (early) or L (late)
- Year: year when the cricket was alive
- ID: individual identifier
- Temp: ambient temperature at sampling, in degrees Celsius
- Age: age at sampling, in days
- Sings: whether the individual was singing at sampling (1) or not (0)

## Data collection and interpretation

- Observations are recorded per sample and per individual male.
- Emergence category defined relative to population median emergence timing.
