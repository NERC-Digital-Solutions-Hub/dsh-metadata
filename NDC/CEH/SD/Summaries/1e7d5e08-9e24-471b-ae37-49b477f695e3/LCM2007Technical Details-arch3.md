# Land Cover Map 2007 (LCM2007) is available in different formats, to suit the requirements of various users.

## Overview
- LCM2007 provides land cover information for the UK and is available in multiple formats to accommodate different user needs.
- The data include metadata describing how the land cover information was derived, enabling users to understand and trust the classifications.

## Data formats

### Vector data
- Most detailed product: a polygon per land parcel with attributes describing land cover and metadata on derivation.
- Minimum mapping unit: 0.5 hectares.
- Supply format: ESRIÂ® Shapefile.
- Coverage: UK.

### Raster data
- Five raster products in total.
- 25 x 25 m raster: gives the most likely Broad Habitat for each pixel.
- Four freely available 1 x 1 km raster products: summarise the 25 x 25 m raster.
- Detailed metadata available in the Land Cover Map 2007 Dataset Documentation.
- Scales: 25 m and 1 km pixel sizes.
- Supply format: GeoTiff.
- Coverage: UK.
