# Land Cover Map 2007

## Overview
- Land Cover Map 2007 provides UK land cover information in multiple formats to suit diverse user needs, with both vector and raster data options.

## Vector data
- Most detailed product: each polygon represents a land parcel with land cover attributes.
- Metadata describes how the information was derived.
- Minimum mappable unit: 0.5 hectares.
- Supply format: ESRI Shapefile.
- Coverage: United Kingdom.

## Raster data
- Five raster products available.
- 25 x 25 m raster gives the most likely Broad Habitat for each pixel.
- Four freely available 1 x 1 km rasters summarize the 25 x 25 m raster.
- Detailed metadata available in the Land Cover Map 2007 Dataset Documentation.
- Scales: 25 m and 1 km pixel size.
- Supply format: GeoTiff.
- Coverage: United Kingdom.

- Supports diverse analytical needs through multiple formats (vector and raster) and varying resolutions.
- Metadata and documentation facilitate data quality assessment, verification, and discoverability.
- Enables integration with other datasets and alignment with data strategy and governance efforts.
- Helpful for planning data assets, establishing standards, and coordinating across teams or partners through common classifications and metadata.
