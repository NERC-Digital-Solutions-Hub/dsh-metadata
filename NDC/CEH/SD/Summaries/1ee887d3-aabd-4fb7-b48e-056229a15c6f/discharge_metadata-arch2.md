# Explanation of data sheet - discharge_data.csv

## Overview
- Sub-hourly discharge data collected from Station S2 on the Siksik stream, Northwest Territories, Canada.
- Geographic context: Longitude 133°26'26"W, Latitude 68°44'17"N, Elevation 85 m.
- Watershed area approximately 1 km²; time zone: Mountain Standard Time (MST).

## Data contents
- Records include: sampling date and time, water temperature (°C), mean stream height (m), and discharge (m³/s).

## Timeframe
- Data cover records from September 2009 and March 2010.

## Data columns and units
- Date_and_Time: Sampling date and time (dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm, 24-hour format).
- Temp_degC: Water temperature (°C).
- Mean_Stream_height_S2m: Stream height (m).
- Discharge_S2_cumsecs: Discharge (m³/s).

## Site details
- Site name: Siksik (Station S2).
- Location: North West Territories, Canada.
