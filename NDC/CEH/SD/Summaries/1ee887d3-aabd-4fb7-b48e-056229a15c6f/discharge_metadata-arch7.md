# Discharge data sheet

## Overview
- Sub-hourly discharge data collected from Station S2, Siksik stream, North West Territories, Canada.
- Variables recorded: water temperature (°C), stream height (m), and discharge (m³/s).
- Records cover September 2009 to March 2010 with precise sampling timestamps.

## Site and context
- Site name: Siksik
- Country: Canada; State/Region: Northwest Territories
- Coordinates: Longitude 133°26'26"W, Latitude 68°44'17"N
- Elevation: 85 m
- Watershed area: approximately 1 km²
- Time zone: Mountain Standard Time

## Data fields and units
- Date_and_Time: Sampling date and time; format dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm (24-hour)
- Temp_degC: Water temperature; degrees Celsius (°C)
- Mean_Stream_height_S2m: Stream height; metres (m)
- Discharge_S2_cumsecs: Discharge; cubic metres per second (m³/s)

## Data format and schema
- Data file: discharge_data.csv
- Columns:
  - Date_and_Time (dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm)
  - Temp_degC (°C)
  - Mean_Stream_height_S2m (m)
  - Discharge_S2_cumsecs (m³/s)
