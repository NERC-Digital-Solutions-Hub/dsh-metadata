# discharge_data.csv

## Purpose and Scope
- Sub-hourly discharge data collected at Station S2, Siksik stream, North West Territories, Canada
- Records cover September 2009 and March 2010
- Variables included: sampling date/time, water temperature, mean stream height, and discharge

## Site and Location Details
- Site name: Siksik
- Country/Region: Canada; North West Territories
- Coordinates: Longitude 133°26'26" W, Latitude 68°44'17" N
- Elevation: 85 m
- Watershed area: approximately 1 km²
- Time zone: Mountain Standard Time

## Data Columns and Units
- Date_and_Time: Sampling date and time; format dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm (24-hour)
- Temp_degC: Water temperature; Degrees Celsius [°C]
- Mean_Stream_height_S2m: Stream height; metres [m]
- Discharge_S2_cumsecs: Discharge; metres cubed per second [m³/s]

## Materials and Methods
- Sub-hourly discharge data collected from Station S2, Siksik stream
- Location details: NW Territories, Canada (Longitude 133°26'26" W; Latitude 68°44'17" N; Elevation 85 m; ~1 km² watershed; MST)
- Records include: sampling date/time (dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm, 24-hour format), water temperature (°C), stream height (m), and discharge (m³/s)

## Data Handling and Metadata
- Data sheet explanation: discharge_data.csv
- Column headings and corresponding units/formats:
  - Date_and_Time: dd/mm/yyyy hh:mm (24-hour)
