# Land Cover Map 2007 (LCM2007) is available in different formats, to suit the requirements of various users.

## Overview
- LCM2007 provides multiple formats to meet diverse user needs with UK coverage.

## Data Formats and Resolution
- Vector data
  - Most detailed product: polygons representing land parcels with attributes for land cover and metadata on derivation.
  - Minimum mapping unit: 0.5 hectares.
  - Supply format: ESRI Shapefile.
- Raster data
  - Five raster products in total.
  - A 25 x 25 m raster gives the most likely Broad Habitat per pixel.
  - Four freely available 1 x 1 km rasters summarize the 25 x 25 m data.
  - Detailed raster metadata available in the dataset documentation.
  - Supply format: GeoTiff.
- Coverage: UK for both vector and raster formats.
