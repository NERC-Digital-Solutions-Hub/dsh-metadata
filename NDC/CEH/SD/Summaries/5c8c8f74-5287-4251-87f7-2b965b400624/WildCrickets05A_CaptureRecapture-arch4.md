# WildCrickets05A-CaptureRecapture Description of content

- Overview
  - Describes the basic information collected for each male adult cricket tagged in the population for capture-recapture studies.
  - Includes the following traits: Year, ID, Birth, Death, and 2-99 (Recapture days).

- Data fields and definitions
  - Year: The year when the cricket was alive.
  - ID: Unique individual identification.
  - Birth: Day of birth relative to the day of birth of the first adult cricket emerging that year; zeros indicate unknown birth date.
  - Death: Date of death relative to the date of birth of the first adult cricket emerging that year.
  - 2-99: Recapture days (the days associated with recapture events).
