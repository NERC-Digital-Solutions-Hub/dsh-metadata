# Interview guideline

- Guidelines with questions in Portuguese prepared for interviews with different actor groups.

- Training of assistant/translator:
  - Lead researcher and one assistant/translator conduct most exercises.
  - Assistant trained on interview objective and question delivery before each exercise.
  - Later, the trained assistant may conduct some exercises independently.

- Triangulation and focusing:
  - Repeated discussions with different groups to validate information.
  - Context from interviews, conversations, and focus groups helps refine interview focus.

- Composition of group:
  - Village leader typically gathers participants.
  - Focus groups include both men and women; everyone is invited to contribute.

- Translation:
  - Researcher is accompanied by a translator/assistant fluent in the local language.
  - Questions translated between Portuguese and Elomwe as needed.

- Note taking:
  - Notes taken by the lead researcher and the assistant.

- Discussion:
  - After each interview, information discussed with the assistant or a colleague to improve understanding.
  - Clarification sought from locals if needed.
