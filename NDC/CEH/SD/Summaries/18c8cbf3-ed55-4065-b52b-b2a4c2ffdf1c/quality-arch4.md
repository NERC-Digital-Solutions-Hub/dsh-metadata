# Interview guideline

- Purpose and language
  - Guidelines with questions prepared in Portuguese for interviews with different actor groups.

- Team roles and training
  - Lead researcher works with one assistant/translator.
  - Assistant trained on interview objectives and question delivery; later may conduct exercises independently.

- Triangulation and focus
  - Repeated discussions across diverse groups to validate information.
  - Context from interviews, conversations, and focus groups helps sharpen interview focus.

- Group composition
  - Village leader typically assembles a group as requested.
  - Focus groups include both men and women; invites everyone to contribute.

- Translation and language
  - A translator/assistant fluent in the local language accompanies the researcher.
  - Questions translated between Portuguese and Elomwe as needed.

- Note taking
  - Notes recorded by the lead researcher and the assistant.

- Post-interview discussion
  - Information discussed after each interview with the assistant or a colleague to improve understanding.
  - Clarifications sought via locals if needed.
