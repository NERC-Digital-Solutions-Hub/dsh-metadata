# Interview guideline

- Prepared interview guidelines with questions in Portuguese for interviews with different actor groups.
- Training of assistant/translator: led by the lead researcher with one assistant/translator; pre-exercise objective briefing; assistant later conducts some exercises independently.
- Triangulation and focusing: discuss topics repeatedly with different groups to validate information and highlight perspectives; use context from interviews, conversations, and focus groups to refine interview focus.
- Composition of group: village leader gathers a group as requested; focus groups include men and women; everyone is explicitly invited to contribute.
- Translation: researcher accompanied by a translator/assistant fluent in the local language; questions translated between Portuguese and Elomwe as needed.
- Note taking: conducted by the lead researcher and the assistant.
- Discussion: after each interview, information discussed with the assistant or a colleague to improve understanding; clarification sought from locals if needed.
