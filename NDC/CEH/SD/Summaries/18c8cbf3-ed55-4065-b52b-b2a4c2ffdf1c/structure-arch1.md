# Details of data structure

- The dataset contains 19 rtf documents, each with transcripts of interviews conducted in a village. Interviewee names are kept anonymous.

- Focus group discussions (FGDs)
  - FGD_Mahara: 6 small-scale soya producers (2 women, 4 men); ~1 hour; translator present (Portuguese-Elomwe); data: 19.10.2015; transcript in English.
  - FGD_Mcunua: 5 producers (5 men, school coordinator); ~1 hour; translator (Portuguese-Elomwe); data: 10.10.2015; transcript in English.
  - FGD_Natxoa: 5 producers (3 men, 2 women); ~1 hour; translator (Portuguese-Elomwe); data: 19.10.2015; transcript in English.
  - FGD_Nuisse: 4 producers (4 men, secretario); ~1 hour; translator (Portuguese-Elomwe); data: 10.10.2015; transcript in English.
  - FGD_Moquito: 6 producers (all men); ~1 hour; translator (Portuguese-Elomwe); data: 24.10.2015; transcript in English.
  - FGD_Serra: 8 producers (4 men, 4 women); ~1 hour; translator (Portuguese-Elomwe); data: 11.10.2015; transcript in English.
  - FGD_Muhelo: 5 producers (all men); ~1 hour; research assistant led with 33-question catalogue; notes in Elomwe; data: 25.11.2015; transcript in Portuguese and English.
  - FGD_Nakuphi: 8 producers (6 men, 2 women); ~1 hour; notes in Elomwe with 33-question catalogue; data: 25.11.2015; transcript in Portuguese and English.
  - FGD_Namiepe: 6 producers (4 men, 2 women); ~1.5 hours; notes in Elomwe with 33-question catalogue; data: 22.10.2015; transcript in Portuguese and English.
  - FGD_Palacue: 8 producers (5 men, 3 women); ~1 hour; notes in Elomwe with 33-question catalogue; data: 25.11.2015; transcript in Portuguese and English.
  - FGD_emergent farmer: 4 emergent soya farmers (3 men, 1 woman) supported by Technoserve; ~1 hour; research researcher led with translator; notes in Elomwe; data: 11.10.2015; transcript in English.

- Interviews
  - Interview_TechnoServe: single technician from TechnoServe; open interview ~1 hour; date 14.07.2015; conducted in Portuguese; transcript in English.
  - Interviews_trader1: traders with buying points along main roads; ~10 minutes each; conducted in Portuguese; data dates 11.07.2015â€“16.07.2015; transcript in English.
  - Interviews_trader2: two informal traders in Gurue; ~20 minutes each; conducted in Portuguese; dates 22.10.2015 and 03.12.2015; transcripts in English.
  - Interview_trader.large: manager of one larger trading company; questions answered in Portuguese; dates 08.12.2015 and 03.12.2015; transcripts in English and Portuguese (original).
  - Interview_company1: management of a private soya producing company; semi-structured, ~1.5 hours; date 11.10.2015; conducted in English.
  - Interview_company2: management of another private soya company; semi-structured, ~1.0 hours; date 16.07.2015; conducted in English.
  - Interview_company3: management of another private soya company; semi-structured, ~1.5 hours; date 08.07.2015; conducted in English.
  - Interview_SDAE: informal communication with a SDAE Gurue technician; Portuguese; date 16.07.2015; transcript in English.

- Language and translation notes
  - Many FGDs used a translator (Portuguese-Elomwe); some notes were taken in Elomwe and later translated to Portuguese and English.
  - Some transcripts are originally in English; others include Portuguese originals with English translations.
  - Anonymity of participants is maintained throughout transcripts.

- Data collection timeline and scope
  - Data collection conducted in 2015 (July to December) across multiple villages.
  - Interviews cover producers (small-scale and emergent), traders, company management, technicians, and a development/program partner (Technoserve/SDAE).

- Metadata and structure
  - Each document corresponds to a specific group or interview context within a village.
  - Transcripts vary in length and depth; some utilize a 33-question catalogue for FGDs.
  - Files include explicit dates, participant roles, languages, and whether notes were translated.
