# Topographic survey conducted at the Rigg Foot plot, Sourhope

## Overview
- Topographic survey conducted at the Rigg Foot plot, Sourhope.
- Performed by Mick Whelan and Stuart Bradley (Department of Environmental Science, University of Stirling).
- Dates: April 4–6 and May 11–12, 2000.

## Methods and Software
- Instrument: Leica Geosystems Total Station EDM.
- Interpolation and contouring: Liscad Plus Surveying and Engineering Environment v4.0.

## Data structure and markers
- Key elements:
  - Treatment boundary marked with +
  - Sub-plot marker peg indicated as - (some peg locations may be missing if peg was not easy to locate)
  - Smaller dots indicate individual survey points (these will be removed from definitive versions)
- Note: Some peg locations may be missing due to visibility or accessibility.

## Grid and sample points
- A grid overlay with 0.5 m cells is superimposed to allow approximate location of sample points.
- Sample points are listed as 01–40, with 31 missing from the sequence in the provided notes.

## Deliverables and notes
- Outputs include topographic data and contours derived from the survey.
- The smaller survey points are intended for interim use; they will be removed in definitive versions.
