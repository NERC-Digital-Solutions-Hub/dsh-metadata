# Topographic survey conducted at the Rigg Foot plot, Sourhope

## Overview
- Topographic survey conducted at the Rigg Foot plot, Sourhope.
- Researchers: Mick Whelan and Stuart Bradley (Department of Environmental Science, University of Stirling).
- Dates: April 4–6, 2000 and May 11–12, 2000.

## Methods and equipment
- Instrument: Leica Geosystems Total Station EDM.
- Data processing: Interpolation and contouring performed using Liscad Plus Surveying and Engineering Environment v4.0 software.
