# PRIMARY Dyfnant Forest draining in to Llyn Vyrnwy

## Overview
- Environmental monitoring dataset focusing on hydrochemistry in plantation spruce forest catchments with brown earth soils in Dyfnant Forest.
- Components include rainfall, ephemeral streams, and boreholes feeding into Afon Hirnant and Llyn Vyrnwy.

- Data verification and quality assurance: standardized collection points and consistent dating across rainfall, streams, and boreholes.
- Standardised outputs: inputs suitable for reports, maps, and charts to assess environmental health and policy performance over time.
- Data accessibility: emphasis on storing and uploading datasets to appropriate portals to enable wider access and reuse.
- Opportunities for integration: designed to be combined with other relevant data to increase dataset value beyond single-use insights.
