# Social survey of learning preferences and knowledge management methods in Puding County, China, 2018

## Experimental design and sampling
- Social survey conducted through interviews with local farmers and leaders in Puding County, China, in 2018.
- Purpose: understand learning preferences and current knowledge management (KM) methods used in China.
- Language: interviews conducted in Chinese.

## Data collection methods
- Interviews were recorded and transcribed after with participants’ agreement (consent).
