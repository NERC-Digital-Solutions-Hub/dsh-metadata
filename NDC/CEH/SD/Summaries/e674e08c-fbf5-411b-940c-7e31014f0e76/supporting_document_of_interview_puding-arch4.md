# Social survey in Puding County, China (2018) - Learning preferences and knowledge management methods

## Purpose
- Understand learning preferences of local farmers and leaders in Puding County, China (2018).
- Identify current knowledge management (KM) methods used in China.
- Conducted in Chinese; interviews used to gather data.

## Methods
- Experimental design/sampling regime mentioned but not detailed.
- Data collection via interviews; sessions recorded and transcribed with participants' consent.
- No fieldwork or laboratory instrumentation reported; calibration steps not applicable.

## Data content and structure
- Data consist of transcription of interview data.
- Dataset comprises 31 .rtf files:

  - Jinhe_x.rtf where x = 1-8 (17 - 21 kb)
