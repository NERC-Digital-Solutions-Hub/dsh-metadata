# Social survey of learning preference and current KM methods used in China

- Overview
  - A social survey conducted in 2018 in Puding County, China, aimed at understanding participants' learning preferences and the current knowledge management (KM) methods used in China.

- Experimental design / Sampling regime
  - Interviews conducted with local farmers and leaders in Puding County.
  - Language: Chinese.
  
- Collection methods
  - Interviews were recorded and subsequently transcribed with participants' agreement.
