# Experimental design/Sampling regime

- Study scope: A social survey conducted in 2018 in Puding County, China, using interviews with local farmers and leaders to understand learning preferences and current KM methods, conducted in Chinese.
- Data collection: Interviews were recorded and transcribed afterwards with participants’ agreement.
- Data units: Transcriptions of interview data.
- Data structure and files: Dataset consists of 31 .rtf files:
  - Chenqi_x.rtf where x = 1–7 (16–55 kb)
  - Houzhai_x.rtf where x = 1–10 (15–22 kb)
  - Jinhe_x.rtf where x = 1–8 (17–21 kb)
  - Maguan_x.rtf where x = 1–5 (15–19 kb)
- Calibration and analytical methods: Not applicable (N/A).
- Quality control: Not applicable (N/A).
