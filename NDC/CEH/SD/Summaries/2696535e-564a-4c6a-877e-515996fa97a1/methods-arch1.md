# Signatures of increasing environmental stress in bumblebee wings over the past century: Insights from museum specimens

## Overview
- The Methods section directs readers to the full methodological details and analyses in the cited paper and its supplementary material.
- The study aims to identify signatures of increasing environmental stress in bumblebee wings over the past century using museum specimens.

## Data sources
- Primary data derived from museum specimens of bumblebees.
- Temporal scope spans the past century.

## Methods and analyses
- Detailed methods and analyses are provided in Arce*, Cantwell-Jones* et al. 2022.
- Supplementary material accompanies the paper, offering additional methodological detail.

## Access and replication
- For understanding or replicating the work, refer to the 2022 Journal of Animal Ecology paper and its associated supplementary materials.
