# Methods

- This document provides only a pointer to the detailed methods and analyses, directing readers to the main paper for full methodological details.
- Primary source for methods: Arce*, Cantwell-Jones* et al. 2022, Signatures of increasing environmental stress in bumblebee wings over the past century: Insights from museum specimens, Journal of Animal Ecology.
- Supplementary materials accompany the paper, offering additional methodological detail and analyses.
- Study focus: using museum specimens to identify signatures of environmental stress on bumblebee wing morphology over about a century, leveraging historical data from archived collections.
- Implications for data leaders: emphasizes the importance of robust documentation, external sources for methods, and reproducibility; to implement similar approaches, consult the cited paper and its supplements for data collection protocols, measurement techniques, quality control, and analytical workflows.
