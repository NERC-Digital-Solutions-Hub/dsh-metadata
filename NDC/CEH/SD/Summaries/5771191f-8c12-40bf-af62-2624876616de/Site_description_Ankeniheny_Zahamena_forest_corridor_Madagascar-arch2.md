# DATA HEADER INFORMATION for Site_description_Ankeniheny_Zahamena_forest_corridor_Madagascar.csv

## Purpose and scope
- Documents the topographical characteristics of each sampling site within the Ankeniheny-Zahamena forest corridor, Madagascar.
- Part of the ESPA program (Project P4GES).
- Includes a DOI and requires acknowledgement of the Laboratoire des Radio Isotopes, Université d'Antananarivo for products derived from these data.

## Data content and structure
- site_ID: P4GES site code; text string
- ZOI: Zone of Interest; categorical (ZOI1: Lakato, ZOI2: Andasibe, ZOI3: Anjahamana, ZOI4: Didy)
- Location: sampling point location; text string
- LU (Land use): type of land use; categorical (Closed Canopy, Tree Savoka, Shrub Savoka, Degraded Land)
- Type: Transect or Plot; categorical (Plot/Transect)
- X: Longitude (decimal degrees, WGS84); numeric
- Y: Latitude (decimal degrees, WGS84); numeric
- Altitude: Elevation in meters; numeric
- Precision: GPS precision in meters; numeric
- slope_up: Slope upward measurement; degrees; numeric
- slope_down: Slope downward measurement; degrees; numeric
- topographic_position: Position on slope (Top, middle, bottom); text
- age_of_deforest: Estimated age of deforestation; years; numeric
- age_of_fallow: Estimated age of fallows; years; numeric
- Historic: Historic context based on local interviews; text

- Field resources and instruments noted where applicable (e.g., GPS eTrex 30 Garmin; botanist; local guides)

## Data collection, quality, and use
- Data are gathered from established sources and field interviews where relevant.
- Processes include verification, quality assurance, cleaning, and transformation.
- Analyses follow standardised methods to produce outputs such as site-level topographical summaries.
- Datasets are stored and uploaded to appropriate data portals.
