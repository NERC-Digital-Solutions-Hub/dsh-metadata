# Surface temperature, surface oxygen, water clarity, water chemistry and phytoplankton chlorophyll a data from Windermere North Basin, UK, 2021 -2022

## Overview
- Ongoing monitoring dataset with fortnightly sampling at the Windermere North Basin, Cumbria, England.
- Timeframe: January 2021 to end of 2022.
- Data cover surface temperature, surface oxygen saturation, Secchi depth, water chemistry, and phytoplankton chlorophyll a.

## Measured Variables and Units
- TEMP: surface temperature in °C
- OXYG: surface oxygen saturation in % air-saturation
- SECC: Secchi depth in metres
- ALKA: alkalinity in µg CaCO3 per litre
- pH: (no unit specified in document)
- NH4N: ammonium in µg N per litre
- NO3N: nitrate in µg N per litre
- PO4P: soluble reactive phosphate in µg P per litre
- TOTP: total phosphorus in µg P per litre
- SIO2: dissolved reactive silicon in µg per litre
- TOCA: phytoplankton chlorophyll a in µg per litre
- Water samples are integrated from 0 to 7 m

## Sampling Regime and Collection Methods
- Measurements taken from a boat at a buoy located at the deepest part of the lake.
- Data provided in a CSV with columns: Date, Variable, Value, Sign(if<LOD).
- Water samples collected and analyzed on a fortnightly basis.

## Data Quality and Validation
- The presented data are raw and not yet quality controlled or calibrated.
- Double entered and visually checked for accuracy.
- Missing data attributed to weather, COVID-19 restrictions, or staff shortages.

## Data Structure and Access
- Data format: comma-separated values (CSV) with columns:
  - Date
  - Description of Variable
  - Value
  - Sign(if<LOD) indicating values below detection limit
- Data cover from January 2021 to December 2022.
- Data collection and validation supported by specific NERC programs (see Funding section).
