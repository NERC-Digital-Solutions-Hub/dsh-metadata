# Countryside Survey 1978: vegetation plot data, Great Britain

## Overview
- Dataset documenting vegetation from Countryside Survey conducted in 1978 (and 1977) across Great Britain.
- Consists of two CSV files: one with plot metadata and one with species lists per plot.
- Produced under the Countryside Survey project and © NERC - Centre for Ecology & Hydrology.
- Data are intended for monitoring environmental conditions and supporting policy-relevant analysis over time.

## Data Files and Structure
- Vegetation Plot - Plot Information 1978.csv
  - YEAR: Survey year
  - SQUARE_ID: Survey square identifier
  - PLOT_ID: Plot identifier
  - PLOT_TYPE: Type of plot
  - COUNTRY: England (ENG), Scotland (SCO), or Wales (WAL)
  - ENV_ZONE_2007: Environmental Zone number (2007 version)
  - EZ_DESC_07: Environmental Zone description (2007 version)
- Vegetation Plot - Species List 1978.csv
  - YEAR: Survey year
  - SQUARE_ID: Survey square identifier
  - PLOT_ID: Plot identifier
  - AMALG_PTYPE: Plot type
  - BRC_NUMBER: Species identifier
  - BRC_NAMES: Species name
  - TOTAL_COVER: Percent cover in the whole plot
- Species nomenclature follows Stace, C. (1997) New Flora of the British Isles.

## Taxonomy and Provenance
- Nomenclature and species codes adhere to established references (Stace, 1997).
- Field methodology and related metadata are documented in the Field Handbook 1978.

## Data Use, Quality, and Monitoring Context
- Data are part of a standardized approach to environmental monitoring, including verification, quality assurance, cleaning, and transformation.
- Outputs support consistent assessment of environmental health against standards and can be presented via reports, maps, and charts.
- Datasets are intended to be stored and uploaded to appropriate data portals to facilitate reuse.
