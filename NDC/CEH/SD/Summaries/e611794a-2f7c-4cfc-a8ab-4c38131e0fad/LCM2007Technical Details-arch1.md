# Land Cover Map 2007 (LCM2007) is available in different formats, to suit the requirements of various users.

- Vector data
  - Most detailed product: each polygon represents a land cover parcel with attributes describing land cover and metadata describing derivation (see Land Cover Map 2007 Dataset Documentation)
  - Scale: Minimum Mapable Unit for land cover parcels: 0.5 ha
  - Supply format: ESRI Shapefile
  - Coverage: UK

- Raster data
  - Five raster products: one 25 x 25 m raster for the most likely Broad Habitat per pixel; four freely available 1 x 1 km rasters that summarize the 25 x 25 m raster
  - Scale: 25 m and 1 km pixel size
  - Supply format: GeoTiff
  - Coverage: UK
