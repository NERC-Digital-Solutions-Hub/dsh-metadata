# Land Cover Map 2007 (LCM2007) is available in different formats, to suit the requirements of various users.

- Overview
  - LCM2007 is provided in both vector and raster formats to meet diverse user needs.
  - Coverage: UK for both formats.

- Vector data
  - Most detailed product: each polygon represents a land parcel with land cover attributes and metadata describing how the information was derived.
  - Minimum mapped unit: 0.5 hectares.
  - Supply format: ESRI Shapefile.
  - Coverage: UK.

- Raster data
  - Five raster products available.
  - 25 x 25 m raster: assigns the most likely Broad Habitat to each pixel.
  - Four freely available 1 x 1 km rasters summarize the 25 x 25 m raster.
  - Detailed metadata available in the Land Cover Map 2007 Dataset Documentation.

- Scales and formats
  - Pixel sizes: 25 m and 1 km.
  - Raster format: GeoTiff.
