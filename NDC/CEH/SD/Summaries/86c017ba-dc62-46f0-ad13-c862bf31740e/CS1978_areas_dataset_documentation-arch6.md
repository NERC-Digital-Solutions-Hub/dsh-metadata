# Landscape area data 1978 [Countryside Survey], Great Britain

## Overview
- Data from the Countryside Survey covering Great Britain, based on 1978 field survey.
- Contains areas of Broad and Priority Habitats across 256 1km squares.
- Each record represents habitat area within a 1km square, reported in square metres.

## Data schema (Columns)
- SQUARE: 1km square sampling site code (text)
- YEAR: Year of survey (number)
- ID: Landscape area polygon identifier (unique within a year)
- BROAD_HABITAT: Broad Habitat code
- BROAD_HABITAT_NAME: Broad or Priority Habitat (text)
- AREA: Area of habitat within the 1km polygon (square metres)
- LAND_CLASS90: ITE Land Class 1990 (numeric)
- COUNTRY: Country where the square is located (England ENG, Scotland SCO, Wales WAL)
- COUNTY: County (Eng & Wal) or Council (Sco)
- EZ_DESC_07: Countryside Survey Environmental Zone description

## Geographical scope and units
- Coverage: Great Britain, across 256 1km squares
- Spatial unit: 1km square polygons
- Habitat area units: square metres
