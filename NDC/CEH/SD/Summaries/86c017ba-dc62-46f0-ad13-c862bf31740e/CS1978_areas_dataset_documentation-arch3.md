# Landscape area data 1978 [Countryside Survey], Great Britain

## Overview
- Dataset from the Countryside Survey for landscape area features in 1978 across Great Britain.
- Contains areas of Broad and Priority Habitats surveyed in 1978 within 256 1km squares.
- Source: Countryside Survey, NERC - Centre for Ecology & Hydrology (CEH).
- Acknowledgement and copyright requirements: data must be acknowledged as Countryside Survey data owned by NERC CEH; full acknowledgment text provided for all copies, publications, and presentations.

## Dataset contents and structure
- File: LANDSCAPE_AREA_FEATURE_HAB_1978.csv
- Columns and meanings:
  - SQUARE: 1km square sampling site code (text)
  - YEAR: Year of survey (number)
  - ID: Landscape area polygon identifier (unique within a year)
  - BROAD_HABITAT: Broad Habitat code
  - BROAD_HABITAT_NAME: Broad or Priority Habitat (text)
  - AREA: Area of habitat within the 1km polygon (square metres)
  - LAND_CLASS90: ITE Land Class 1990 (reference classifications)
  - COUNTRY: Country within GB (England ENG, Scotland SCO, Wales WAL)
  - COUNTY: County or Council area (Eng & Wal) or in Scotland (EZ_DESC_07)
  - EZ_DESC_07: Countryside Survey Environmental Zone description
- The dataset ties habitat area measurements to both a land classification framework and established habitat nomenclature.

## Data provenance, metadata, and classification context
- Uses Broad Habitat and ITE Merlewood Land Classification frameworks:
  - BROAD_HABITAT_NAME references definitions (e.g., from Jackson 2000 guidance on Biodiversity Broad Habitat Classification).
  - LAND_CLASS90 refers to ITE Land Classification of Great Britain 1990.
- Related methodological and classification references are provided (see Publications & References).
- Links and publications provide background on how 1978 data contribute to measuring habitat change over time and national estimates from the 1km sample squares.
