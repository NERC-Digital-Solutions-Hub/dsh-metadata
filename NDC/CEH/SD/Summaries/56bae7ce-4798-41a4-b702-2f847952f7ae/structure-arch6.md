# Details of data structure

## Overview
- Dataset comprises 12 RTf documents containing interview transcripts from anonymous villages.
- Village identities are anonymized; real names can be obtained from the data owner on request.

## Data files and contents
- village_camps
  - Four interviews with charcoal camp workers in the Mabalane forest
  - Approximately 10 minutes each
  - Leadership: two researchers
  - Translator: Portuguese-Changana
  - Interview dates: 08.07.2014 – 25.07.2014
- villageA_all.interviews
  - Seven semi-structured interviews with villagers involved in charcoal production
  - Notes from informal conversation
  - Leader: one researcher
  - Translator: native speaker (Portuguese ⇄ Changana)
  - Interview dates: 18.08.2014 – 20.08.2014
- villageB_all.interviews
  - One focus group discussion and one semi-structured interview
  - Leader: one researcher
  - Translator: native speaker (Portuguese ⇄ Changana)
  - Interview date: 01.09.2014
- villageC_all.interviews
  - One focus group discussion, four semi-structured interviews, notes from informal communication
  - Leader: one researcher
  - Translator: native speaker (Portuguese ⇄ Changana)
  - Interview dates: 25.08.2014 – 29.08.2014
- villageD_focus.group
  - One focus group discussion with charcoal producers
  - Leader: one researcher
  - Translator: native speaker (Portuguese ⇄ Changana)
  - Interview date: 18.09.2014
- villageE_focus.group
  - One focus group with charcoal producers
  - Two semi-structured interviews (forester and association president)
  - Seven short interviews with workers
  - Leader: one researcher
  - Translator: native speaker (Portuguese ⇄ Changana)
  - Interview dates: 11.09.2014 – 17.09.2014
- villageF_focus.group
  - One focus group with charcoal producers
  - Leader: one researcher
  - Translator: native speaker (Portuguese ⇄ Changana)
  - Interview date: 19.09.2014
- villageG_interview.fiscal
  - One semi-structured interview with the community forester
  - Leader: one researcher
  - Translator: native speaker (Portuguese ⇄ Changana)
  - Interview date: 15.09.2014
- villageH.I_trucks
  - Interviews with truck drivers exiting the forest with loaded charcoal
  - Approximately 10 minutes each
  - Language: Portuguese
  - Interview dates: 22–24.07.2014 and 18–20.09.2014
- villageH_focus.group
  - One focus group with members of a charcoal association
  - Leader: one researcher
  - Translator: native speaker (Portuguese ⇄ Changana)
  - Interview date: 20.09.2014
- villageH_interview.fiscal
  - Informal communication with a district forester
  - Language: Portuguese
  - Interview date: 22.07.2014
- villageI_interview.fiscal
  - Informal communication with a district forester
  - Language: Portuguese
  - Interview date: 06.07.2014

## Interview methods and participants
- Mix of semi-structured interviews, focus groups, and informal communications
- Participants include charcoal producers, community foresters, association presidents, and truck drivers
- Each file indicates a leading researcher and, where applicable, a translator

## Language and translation
- Translators are typically native speakers, providing Portuguese ↔ Changana translation
- Some interviews conducted entirely in Portuguese (e.g., truck drivers)

## Temporal coverage
- Interviews conducted between July and September 2014
- Specific date ranges provided per file, spanning late July to late September 2014

## Anonymity and access
- Village names are anonymized in the dataset
- Access to real village names can be requested from the data owner

## Data quality and notes
- Data comprises transcripts in RTf format, with varying interview types and lengths
- Some entries include notes from informal communications
- Language and translation consistency noted via designated translators and native speakers
