# 1) Interview guideline

- Purpose and setup
  - Guidelines for interviews with different actor groups; questions prepared in Portuguese.

- Training of assistant/translator
  - Prefer to work with the same assistant/translator consistently; briefed before each exercise about objectives and how to ask questions.

- Triangulation and focusing
  - Repeated discussions across groups to validate information and highlight perspectives; use context from interviews and focus groups to focus inquiries.

- Composition of group
  - Village leader typically gathers participants; focus groups include men and women; all invited to contribute.

- Translation
  - Researcher is accompanied by a translator/assistant who speaks the local language; questions translated between Portuguese and Changana as needed.

- Note taking
  - Lead researcher is responsible for taking notes.

- Discussion
  - After each interview, information discussed with assistant or a colleague to improve understanding; seek clarification from locals when needed.

- Transcript in English
  - Notes are written up in English after returning to the office.

- Back-up
  - Multiple backups prepared for all documents.
