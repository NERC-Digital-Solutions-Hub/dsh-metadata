# March Classification Metadata

## Overview
- Classifications were carried out using a maximum likelihood algorithm.
- For full methods, see 'Methods_overview'.

## Definitions
- Edge pixels: Pixels at the edge of floral unit clusters or in the center of small clusters where they appear mixed with other features.
- Pure pixels: Pixels deeper into the center of floral unit clusters, less likely to be mixed with other features.

## Training Set Variants
- March_3cm_classification1
- March_3cm_classification2
- Training set composition:
  - Blackthorn edge and pure pixels both included within the training set.
  - Or, only blackthorn pure pixels included within the training set.
