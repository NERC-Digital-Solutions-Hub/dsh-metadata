# March Classification Metadata

## Overview
- All classifications were carried out using a maximum likelihood algorithm.
- For full methods, see 'Methods_overview'.

## Definitions
- Edge pixels: Pixels at the edge of floral unit clusters or in the center of small clusters where the pixel appears mixed with other features.
- Pure pixels: Pixels further into the centre of floral unit clusters that are less likely to be mixed with other features.

## Training set variants
- March_3cm_classification1
- March_3cm_classification2
- Blackthorn edge and pure pixels both included within the training set.
- Only blackthorn pure pixels included within the training set.
