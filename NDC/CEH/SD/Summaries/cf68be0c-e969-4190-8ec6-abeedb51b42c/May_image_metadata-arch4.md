# May Image Metadata

- Imagery details: acquired at a farm in Northamptonshire, UK on 14–15 May 2019 at 3 cm and 7 cm spatial resolutions.
- Sensor and platform: Hasselblad A6D-100c (50 mm) cameras with Bayer filters mounted on a Sky Arrow 650 manned aircraft.
- Data characteristics: each image is a 16-bit orthomosaic with coordinate reference system OSGB 1936 / British National Grid (EPSG: 27700).
- Classification data (3 cm): May_3cm is a TIFF with clipped 4-band 3 cm resolution image used for classifications; bands are blue (B0), green (B1), red (B2), and infrared (B3).
- Original multi-band data: 14_05_2019_6_Band_3 contains the original 3 cm data for May across 6 bands; 14_05_2019_6_Band_7_BIL corresponds to the 7 cm data across 6 bands.
- Classification data (7 cm): May_7cm is a TIFF with clipped 4-band 7 cm resolution image used for classifications (B0–B3 as blue, green, red, infrared).
- Data provenance: references to the original 7 cm 6-band image for May and guidance to consult Table_band_data in the main "Mapping nectar-rich floral resources for pollinators" folder for individual band data.
