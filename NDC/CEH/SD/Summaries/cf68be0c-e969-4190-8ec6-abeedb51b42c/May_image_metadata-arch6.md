# May Image Metadata

- Summary of imagery collected for mapping nectar-rich floral resources for pollinators in Northamptonshire, UK.
- Acquisition date: 14–15 May 2019.
- Sensor setup: Hasselblad A6D-100c cameras with 50 mm lenses and Bayer filters, mounted on a Sky Arrow 650 aircraft.
- Coverage and projection: 16-bit orthomosaic imagery in OSGB 1936 / British National Grid (EPSG: 27700).

## Data files and formats

- May_3cm
  - TIFF containing a clipped 4-band 3 cm resolution image used for classifications.
  - Bands correspond to blue, green, red, and infrared (B0–B3).
  - See Table_band_data in the Mapping nectar-rich floral resources for pollinators folder for per-band details.
- 14_05_2019_6_Band_3
  - TIFF containing the original 3 cm data for May across 6 bands.
  - 16-bit imagery; per-band data available in Table_band_data.
- May_7cm
  - TIFF containing a clipped 4-band 7 cm resolution image used for classifications.
  - Bands correspond to blue, green, red, and infrared (B0–B3).
  - See Table_band_data for individual band data.
- 14_05_2019_6_Band_7_BIL
  - TIFF containing the original 7 cm data for May across 6 bands (original dataset).
  - Per-band details available in Table_band_data.

## Usage and context

- The clipped 4-band images (May_3cm and May_7cm) are intended for classification workflows.
- The 6-band originals (14_05_2019_6_Band_3 and 14_05_2019_6_Band_7_BIL) provide full spectral information for deeper analysis.
- All data and band-specific metadata are organized under the Mapping nectar-rich floral resources for pollinators folder, with Table_band_data supplying individual band information.
