# Land Cover Map 2007

## Overview
- Land Cover Map 2007 (LCM2007) is available in different formats to suit the requirements of various users.

## Data formats

- Vector data
  - Most detailed product: each polygon represents a land parcel with attributes describing land cover and metadata describing derivation.
  - Minimum mapable unit for land cover parcels: 0.5 ha.
  - Supply format: ESRI Shapefile.
  - Coverage: UK.
  - Refer to the Land Cover Map 2007 Dataset Documentation for details.

- Raster data
  - Five raster products.
  - A 25 x 25 m raster gives the most likely Broad Habitat for each pixel.
  - Four freely available 1 x 1 km raster products summarize the 25 x 25 m raster.
  - Detailed metadata available in the Land Cover Map 2007 Dataset Documentation.
  - Supply format: GeoTiff.
  - Scales: 25 m and 1 km pixel sizes.
  - Coverage: UK.

## Coverage and scale
- Coverage: United Kingdom (UK).
- Formats cover both fine-scale (0.5 ha vector units; 25 m raster) and coarser summaries (1 km raster).
