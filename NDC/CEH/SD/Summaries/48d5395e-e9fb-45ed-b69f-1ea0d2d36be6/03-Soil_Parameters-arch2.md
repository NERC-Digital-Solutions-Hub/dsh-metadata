# Supporting documentation for 03-Soil_Parameters.csv

- Overview
  - Metadata and field definitions for the 03-Soil_Parameters dataset, to support standardized monitoring of soil properties and ensure data quality, interoperability, and traceability.

- Dataset Fields and Definitions
  - Sample_Code: Unique sample identifier; Units: n/a; Notes: n/a.
  - Sample_Type: General description of sample (e.g., foodstuff group); Units: n/a; Notes: Soil.
  - Location: Name of the location where the sample was collected; Units: n/a; Notes: n/a.
  - Sample_Description: Further description of the sample; Units: n/a; Notes: Soil 0 - 10 cm.
  - Collection_Date: Date of sample collection; Units: dd-mm-yyyy; Notes: n/a.
  - pH: Soil pH in water; Units: n/a; Notes: n/a.
  - Conductivity_uS/cm: Soil conductivity; Units: Î¼S/cm; Notes: n/a.
  - %_Clay: Percentage of clay in soil; Units: %; Notes: n/a.
  - %_Silt: Percentage of silt in soil; Units: %; Notes: n/a.
  - %_Fine_sand: Percentage of fine sand in soil; Units: %; Notes: n/a.
  - %_Coarse_sand: Percentage of coarse sand in soil; Units: %; Notes: n/a.

- Data Quality and Usage
  - Data are verified, quality assured, cleaned, and transformed.
  - Outputs are presented in clear formats such as reports, maps, and charts.
  - Datasets are stored and uploaded to appropriate data portals.

- Alignment with Analysts Monitoring the Environment
  - Provides standardized soil-health data to support assessment of environmental health and policy performance over time.
