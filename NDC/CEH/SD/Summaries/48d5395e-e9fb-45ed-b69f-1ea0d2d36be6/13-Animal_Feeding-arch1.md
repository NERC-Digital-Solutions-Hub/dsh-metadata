# Supporting documentation for 13-Animal_Feeding.csv

- Purpose and scope
  - Metadata describing the fields and data types for the 13-Animal_Feeding.csv dataset.
  - Facilitates data understanding, cleaning, and integration for analyses of animal feeding regimes and intake.

- Variables and definitions
  - Animal: General type of animal.
  - Location: Name of the location where the sample was collected.
  - Feeding: Description of the feeding regime.
  - Daily_Ingestion: Amount of dry matter ingested by an animal per day.
  - Composition_Animal_Feed: Equation for the weighted composition of the total diet; information about animal feed composition provided by producers.
  - Abbreviations: Based on Sample Codes.

- Measurement details
  - Daily_Ingestion units: kg dry matter per day.
  - Other fields (Animal, Location, Feeding, Composition_Animal_Feed, Abbreviations): Units not applicable (n/a).

- Data provenance and coding
  - Abbreviations are derived from Sample Codes, linking shorthand to dataset records.
  - Composition_Animal_Feed relies on information provided by producers about feed composition.

- Notes and caveats
  - Most fields have no additional notes (n/a).
  - Composition_Animal_Feed relies on producer-provided data; the exact equation is not specified in this documentation.
  - Clear mapping between sample codes and abbreviations is required for consistent interpretation.
