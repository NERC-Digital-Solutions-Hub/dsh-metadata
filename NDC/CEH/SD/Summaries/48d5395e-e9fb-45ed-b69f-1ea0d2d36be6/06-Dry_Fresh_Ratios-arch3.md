# Supporting documentation for 06-Dry_Fresh_Ratios.csv

## Overview
- Provides metadata for the dataset 06-Dry_Fresh_Ratios.csv, detailing the fields, their explanations, units, and notes.
- Fields included cover sample descriptions and key ratio statistics: mean, minimum, and maximum values of the dry-to-fresh mass ratio.
- All relevant units for the ratio fields are unitless; most notes are listed as n/a.

## Field definitions

- Sample_Type
  - Explanation: General description of sample (i.e. foodstuff group)
  - Units: n/a
  - Notes: n/a

- Sample_Description
  - Explanation: Part of the organism analysed (i.e. where whole organism was divided and analysed separately)
  - Units: n/a
  - Notes: n/a

- Mean_Value_Dry/Fresh_Ratio
  - Explanation: Mean value of ratio between dry mass and fresh mass
  - Units: Unitless
  - Notes: n/a

- Min_Value_Dry/Fresh_Ratio
  - Explanation: Minimum value of ratio between dry mass and fresh mass
  - Units: Unitless
  - Notes: n/a

- Max_Value_Dry/Fresh_Ratio
  - Explanation: Maximum value of ratio between dry mass and fresh mass
  - Units: Unitless
  - Notes: n/a

## Metadata and data governance implications
- The document presents a structured approach to metadata, ensuring clear interpretation of each field through Explanation, Units, and Notes.
- Unitless ratio values emphasize the need for careful consistency and documentation to support data sharing and reuse.
- The explicit field descriptions facilitate data quality assurance, comparability across samples, and potential integration into monitoring frameworks.
