# Audible and ultrasound recordings to survey birds and bats on Barro Colorado Island and in the Gamboa forest region, Panama, January 2023

- Overview
  - Dataset of acoustic sounds recorded in Panama during January 2023 to trial the ability of song meter devices to record surrounding audio and ultrasound noise.
  - Aimed at evaluating audible and ultrasound recording capabilities using two devices.

- Devices Used
  - Wildlife Acoustics SM4 (audible recordings)
  - Wildlife Acoustics SM4BAT (ultrasound recordings)
  - Both devices can be deployed for extended periods and configured to sample around sunrise and sunset times.

- Sampling and Scheduling
  - Audible recordings: collected during windows around sunrise/sunset (e.g., 1 hour before sunset to 1 hour after sunset).
  - Ultrasound recordings: collected during daylight hours (between sunrise and sunset; e.g., 30 minutes before sunset to 30 minutes after sunrise).

- Data Organization and Naming
  - Audible recordings: folders named yy_mm_dd_Gamboa or yy_mm_dd_BCI, indicating the region and survey date.
  - Ultrasound recordings: folders named Bat_yy_mm_dd, with the date representing the survey start date.
  - The date component (yy_mm_dd) corresponds to when the survey began.
