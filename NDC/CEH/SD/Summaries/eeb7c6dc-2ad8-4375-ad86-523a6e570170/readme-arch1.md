# Audible and ultrasound recordings to survey birds and bats on Barro Colorado Island and in the Gamboa forest region, Panama, January 2023

## Overview
- Dataset of acoustic sounds recorded in Panama in January 2023 to trial the ability of song meter devices to capture audible and ultrasound noise.
- Aimed at assessing equipment performance for environmental acoustic surveys.

## Devices Used
- Wildlife Acoustics SM4 for audible recordings.
- Wildlife Acoustics SM4Bat for ultrasound recordings.
- Both devices support long deployments and scheduled sampling around sunrise and sunset.

## Recording Locations and Period
- four sites: Barro Colorado Island (BCI) and the Gamboa Forest region.

## Recording Windows
- Audible recordings: time intervals around sunrise/sunset (e.g., 1 hour before sunset to 1 hour after sunset is given as an example).
- Ultrasound recordings: intervals within the daylight window (e.g., 30 minutes before sunset to 30 minutes after sunrise).

## Data Organization and Naming Conventions
- Audible data: folders named yy_mm_dd_Gamboa or yy_mm_dd_BCI, indicating date and region.
- Ultrasound data: folders named Bat_yy_mm_dd, with yy_mm_dd matching the survey start date.
- For both types, the segment yy_mm_dd corresponds to the survey start date.

## Metadata and Access
- Metadata.csv contains per-deployment details (device, survey start/end times, longitude, latitude, etc.).
- Comprehensive dataset details available on the EIDC catalogue.
