# Metadata for 'bat_morpho_razgour_2015-2020.csv'

## Overview
- Morphological and life-history data for three bat species (Barbastella barbastellus, Plecotus auritus, Myotis escalerai).
- Collected 2015â€“2020 in England, Spain, and Portugal to study adaptive responses to climate change as part of a NERC IRF project.

## Collection and generation methods
- Captures used mist nets and harp traps at roosts, caves, foraging sites, and near small water bodies.
- Samples collected: wing tissue (biopsies), blood, and feces.
- Bats kept in individual cotton bags during processing; non-target species released immediately; target species released after processing.

## Nature and units of recorded values
- Location: latitude and longitude in WGS84 decimal degrees, resolution ~10 km.
- Sex: F (female) or M (male).
- Age: Adults, Sub-adults, Juveniles.
- Reproductive condition (females): Nulliparous, Pregnant, Lactating, Post-lactating, Nonreproductive.
- Reproductive condition (males): inferred from position/size of testicles.
- Forearm: measurements in millimetres (mm).
- BodyMass: measurements in grams (g).

## Quality control
- All measurements taken by the same experienced person.
- Data checked for outliers and anomalies.

## Details of data structure
- Single CSV file including all three species.
- Empty cells denote missing values.
