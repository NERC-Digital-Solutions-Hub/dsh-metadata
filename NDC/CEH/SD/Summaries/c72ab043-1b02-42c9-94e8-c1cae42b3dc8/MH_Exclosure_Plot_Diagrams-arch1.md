# Plot layout at Little Dun Fell

- The document describes the layout and sampling design for vegetation study across multiple sites, focusing on grazed versus enclosed (fenced) plots and including the Juncus squarrosus grassland.
- Sites referenced include Little Dun Fell, Troutbeckhead, and Hill, with layouts depicted for each.

## Experimental design

- Plots are arranged as 10 by 6 plots, identified by corner posts and gridded into multiple quadrats for sampling.
- Treatments include grazed areas and enclosed (exclusion) areas, with sampling conducted across both to compare effects of grazing.
- The Hill study explicitly notes a not-to-scale diagram, indicating practical field arrangement rather than precise measurement.

- The sampling framework is subdivided into 10 transects, each containing 10 frame positions for point quadrat studies, enabling systematic assessment of vegetation coverage and composition along multiple axes.
