# A 'Reference Site' in the Chernobyl Exclusion Zone: radionuclide and stable element data, and estimated dose rates

## Overview
- Metadata for a dataset from a reference site in the Chernobyl Exclusion Zone, focusing on radionuclide and stable element data and estimated dose rates.
- Source: NERC Environmental Information Data Centre; includes a DOI for traceability.
- Aimed at standardised, time-consistent presentation to support environmental health and policy performance assessment.

## Data files and structure
- Files included: Biota_external_dose.csv, Biota_internal_dose.csv, Biota_total_dose.csv, Biota_total_dose_per_radionuclide.csv.
- Core concepts:
  - Radionuclide examined: Cs-137, Sr-90, Am-240, Pu-239, Pu-240, Pu-238.
  - Dose-rate data presented for external exposure (External_Dose_Rate_microGy_h-1) with statistical summaries.
  - Statistical data provided: Mean, Variance, 5th and 95th Percentiles, Median, Minimum, Maximum, 25th and 75th Percentiles.
  - Units: microGray per hour (Î¼Gy h-1).
