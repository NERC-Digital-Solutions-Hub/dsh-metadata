# A 'Reference Site' in the Chernobyl Exclusion Zone: radionuclide and stable element data, and estimated dose rates

## Overview
- Metadata describing a dataset on radionuclide and stable element data and estimated dose rates for a reference site in the Chernobyl Exclusion Zone.
- Published by the NERC Environmental Information Data Centre.
- DOI: https://doi.org/10.5285/ae02f4e89486-4b47-93ef-e49dd9ddecd4.

## Data files and contents
- Files mentioned: Biota_external_dose.csv, Biota_internal_dose.csv, Biota_total_dose.csv, Biota_total_dose_per_radionuclide.csv.
- Radionuclides examined: Cs-137, Sr-90, Am-240, Pu-239, Pu-240, or Pu-238.
- External dose rate statistics available: Mean, Variance, 5th Percentile, 95th Percentile, Median, Minimum, Maximum, 25th Percentile, 75th Percentile.
- External dose rate unit: microGray per hour (microGy h-1).

## Radionuclides and dose rate statistics
- External dose rate data are provided per radionuclide with associated statistical summaries (mean, percentiles, etc.).
- Data are structured to support analysis of dose rates across different radionuclides.
