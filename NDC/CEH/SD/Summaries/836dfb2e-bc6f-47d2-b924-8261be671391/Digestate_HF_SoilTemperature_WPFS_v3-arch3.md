# Digestate_HF_SoilTemperature_WFPS_v1.csv

## Overview
- Supplementary documentation detailing the data structure for the CSV file Digestate_HF_SoilTemperature_WFPS_v1.csv.
- Part of the supporting documentation for data series described in Supporting_documentation_CINAg_experiments_final_check_v2.1.pdf.
- Data originate from the digestate wheat trial conducted in 2017 at Henfaes Research Center, Bangor University.
- The dataset contains 4 columns and 87,980 rows, recording soil temperature and water-filled pore space (WFPS) at 2.5 cm depth across ten plots.

## Dataset structure
- File: Digestate_HF_SoilTemperature_WFPS_v1.csv
- Columns: 4
- Rows: 87,980
- Plots: 10 plots identified as 5, 6, 8, 10, 15, 18, 24, 28, 29, 30
- Measurements: soil temperature and WFPS at 2.5 cm soil depth
- Context: data collected during the 2017 digestate wheat trial at Henfaes Research Center

## Columns and units
- Time: Date and time of day
- Plot numbers: 5, 6, 8, 10, 15, 18, 24, 28, 29, 30
- Soil_temperature_degrees_celcius: soil temperature at 2.5 cm depth. Units: Degrees Celsius
- WFPS_percent: water-filled pore space at 2.5 cm soil depth. Units: Percent
