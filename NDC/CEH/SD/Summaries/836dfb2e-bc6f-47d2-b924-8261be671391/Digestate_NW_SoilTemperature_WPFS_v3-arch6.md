# Details of data structure to 'Digestate_NF_SoilTemperature_WFPS.csv'

- This document supplements the supporting documentation for data series detailed in 'Supporting_documentation_CINAg_experiments_final_check_v2.1.pdf'.
- Dataset overview:
  - 4 columns and 31,778 rows.
  - Data from the digestate wheat trial 2017, North Wyke Research station, Rothamsted Research.
  - Measurements include soil temperature and water-filled pore space (WFPS) at 2.5 cm soil depth across ten plots.
- Dataset structure (columns, header explanations, and units):
  - Time: Date and time of day.
  - Block: Blocks 1 to 5.
  - Soil_Temperature_degrees_celcius: soil temperature at 2.5 cm depth. Units: Degrees Celsius.
  - WFPS_percent: water-filled pore space at 2.5 cm soil depth. Units: Percent.
