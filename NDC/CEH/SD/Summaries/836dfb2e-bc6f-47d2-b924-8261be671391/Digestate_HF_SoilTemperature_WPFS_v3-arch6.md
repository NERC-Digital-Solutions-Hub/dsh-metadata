# Digestate_HF_SoilTemperature_WFPS_v1.csv

## Overview
- Supplement to the supporting documentation for data series detailed in Supporting_documentation_CINAg_experiments_final_check_v2.1.pdf.
- Provides data structure details for Digestate_HF_SoilTemperature_WFPS_v1.csv.

## Dataset details
- 4 columns and 87,980 rows.
- Source: digestate wheat trial 2017, Henfaes Research Center, Bangor University.
- Variables collected: soil temperature and water-filled pore space (WFPS) at 2.5 cm soil depth.
- Plots sampled: 10 plots with IDs 5, 6, 8, 10, 15, 18, 24, 28, 29, 30.

## Column definitions
- Time: Date and time of day.
- Plot_numbers: 5, 6, 8, 10, 15, 18, 24, 28, 29, 30.
- Soil_temperature_degrees_celcius: soil temperature at 2.5 cm depth. Unit: Degrees Celsius.
- WFPS_percent: water-filled pore space at 2.5 cm soil depth. Unit: Percent.

## Data context and usage
- Enables end users to explore patterns in soil temperature and WFPS across plots and times.
- Supports creation of data products (e.g., dashboards, charts) for self-service analysis.
- Fits within broader data support activities by combining with other related datasets for enhanced interpretation.
