# TREE_Northumbria_Animal_Tissue_Mass

## Overview
- Metadata schema for Northumbria animal tissue mass data.
- Captures species, sample identifiers, tissue type, and mass measurements, with explanatory notes for each field.

## Data Fields and Explanations
- Latin_Species_Name: Latin name of species.
- CEH_Sample_Code: CEH sample codes associated with the animal or sample. Not applicable for estimates of total muscle and total bone.
- CEH_Sample_Description: CEH sample description.
- Tissue: Tissue type.
- Tissue_Fresh_Mass_kg: Tissue fresh mass in kilograms. Not available where mass was not obtained.
- Tissue_Freeze_Dried_Mass_kg: Tissue freeze dried mass in kilograms. Not available where mass was not obtained.
- Fresh_Mass_Dry_Mass_Ratio: Fresh mass to freeze-dried mass ratio of tissue. Not available where a ratio cannot be calculated.
- General_Notes: General notes.
