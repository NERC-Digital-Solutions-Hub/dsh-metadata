## Title

 Integrated abundance and environmental data for river fish and macroinvertebrates in England (1970s-2018)


## Description

Fish and macroinvertebrate abundance data for English rivers from the 1970s to 2018 combined with physical, chemical, habitat and climatic records. The work was based largely around Environment Agency records and was carried out by UKCEH and University of Hull funded by NERC in the ChemPop project NE/S000100/1.
