## Title

Nitrous oxide fluxes and associated measurements from Easter Bush Farm, Central Scotland (2012 – 2013)


## Description

These series comprise of nitrous oxide (N2O) flux measurements and the accompanying data  collected during the UK GHG platform project AC0116 (The INVEN2ORY project). Fluxes were measured using either the flux chamber or eddy covariance methods from Easter Bush Farm (University of Edinburgh), located in the Midlothian area 16 miles south of Edinburgh, Scotland. The work was carried out as part of a PhD project (in partnership with the University of Edinburgh) investigating N2O fluxes from plot to farm scale.
