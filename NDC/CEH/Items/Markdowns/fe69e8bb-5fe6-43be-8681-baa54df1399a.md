## Title

Land Cover Change 1990-2015


## Description

This collection consists of 25m raster Land Cover Change 1990 - 2015 products for the United Kingdom. 

The data sets are produced from the 25m raster versions of LCM1990 and LCM2015 and report Land Cover Change between 1990 and 2015 in six simplified classes. The products consist of five bands:
Band 1 – a raster version of LCM1990 in the six simplified classes;
Band 2 – a raster version of LCM1990 in the six simplified classes;
Band 3 – a binary layer, where 0 means no change between 1990 and 2015 and 1 means change between 1990 and 2015;
Band 4 – shows the 'change from' class;
Band 5 – provides the 'change to' class.

Full details of the Land Cover Change data set are provided in the Land Cover Change data set documentation, which users should consult.
