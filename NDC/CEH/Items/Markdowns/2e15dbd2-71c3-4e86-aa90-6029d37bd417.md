## Title

Areas affected by landslides following Typhoon Parma in Itogon, Philippines, October 2009


## Description

This dataset contains the areas affected by landslides triggered by Typhoon Parma in the area of Itogon (Benguet, Philippines) between the 2nd and 5th October 2009. The polygons were mapped using Google Earth imagery dated 31 December 2003 for pre-event and images and 31 December 2009 for post-event images. The area has an extension of 150 km2.

## Lineage

The polygons of the landslides were mapped by two people. The technique to map them was visual observation of evidences of change before and after the typhoon, that would correspond to the appearance of a landslide. The inventory was checked by two people  afterwards.
