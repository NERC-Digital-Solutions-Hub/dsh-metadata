## Title

Pike 1944-2002


## Description

This series of datasets consists of growth (length) data, survival data and fecundity data for Pike (Esox lucius) from net sampling in Windermere. Lake temperature data is also part of the series. Data collection began in 1944. The data were initially collected by the Freshwater Biological Association (FBA) but have been collected by CEH and its predecessor Institute of Freshwater Ecology (IFE) since 1989.
