## Title

Windermere perch data 1946-2012


## Description

This series of datasets consists of monthly mean inshore surface water temperature (degrees C) data from Windermere, total weekly catch data of mature Perch (Perca fluviatilis) from trapping at Green Tuft, Windermere and weekly mean sizes of Perch from trapping at Green Tuft, Windermere. The data presented were sampled between 1946 and 2012 from Windermere, a lake in northern England. The data were collected by the Freshwater Biological Association (FBA) and then by the Centre for Ecology & Hydrology and its predecessor Institute of Freshwater Ecology (IFE). The files available in this series were used in Ohlberger et al. (2014) Proceedings of the Royal Society B (When phenology matters: Age-size truncation alters population response to trophic mismatch).

## Lineage

The data presented here have been gathered during long term monitoring activity on Windermere, a lake in northern England. Lake temperature has been monitored since 1933 and perch trapping has been carried out since 1943. Monitoring has been undertaken both by the Freshwater Biological Association and by the Centre for Ecology & Hydrology and its predecessor Institute of Freshwater Ecology (IFE). Please see the supporting documentation accompanying each dataset record for further information.
