## Title

Grid-to-Grid model estimates of river flow for Northern Ireland driven by observed data (1980 to 2011)


## Description

Gridded hydrological model river flow estimates on a 1km grid over Northern Ireland for the period Dec 1980 - Nov 2011. The dataset includes monthly mean river flow, annual maxima of daily mean river flow (water years Oct - Sept) and annual minima of 7-day mean river flow (years spanning Dec-Nov) (units: m3/s). The data are provided in gridded netCDF files. There is one file for each variable.

To aid interpretation, three additional spatial datasets are provided: a) digitally-derived catchment areas and b) estimated locations of flow gauging stations both on the 1km x 1km grid and c) a 1km x 1km grid identifying majority lake cells.

The data were produced as part of UK-SCAPE (UK Status, Change And Projections of the Environment, Work Package 2: Case Study - Water) a NERC-funded National Capability Science Single Centre award.

## Lineage

This dataset is a model output from the Grid-to-Grid hydrological model (Bell et al. 2009; [https://doi.org10.1016/j.jhydrol.2009.08.031](https://doi.org10.1016/j.jhydrol.2009.08.031) driven by observed data. The meteorological variables used were: 
 - daily precipitation (CEH-GEAR; [https://doi.org/10.5285/33604ea0-c238-4488-813d-0ad9ab7c51ca](https://doi.org/10.5285/33604ea0-c238-4488-813d-0ad9ab7c51ca)
 - daily min and max temperature (Met Office; [https://doi.org/10.5285/2a62652a4fe6412693123dd6328f6dc8](https://doi.org/10.5285/2a62652a4fe6412693123dd6328f6dc8) 
 - monthly 40km grids of PE for short grass (MORECS; [https://doi.org/10.5194/hess-1-227-1997](https://doi.org/10.5194/hess-1-227-1997)

See accompanying documenation for more details.
