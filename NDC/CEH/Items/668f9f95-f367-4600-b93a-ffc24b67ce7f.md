## Title

Phenological trait data for Moringa oleifera Lam. from Kenya 2020


## Description

This dataset is the first phenological trait data for Moringa oleifera and M. stenopetala trees from provenances collected in Kenya and planted at Ramogi. Trees were measured and scored for survival, height, diameter at breast height, fruiting and damage by three field surveyors.
