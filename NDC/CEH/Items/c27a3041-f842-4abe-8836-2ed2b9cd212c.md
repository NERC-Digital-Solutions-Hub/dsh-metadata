## Title

Data from Building Resilience in Ethiopia's Awassa region to Drought (BREAD) project


## Description

This collection contains data from the Building Resilience in Ethiopia's Awassa region to Drought (BREAD). This project combined data collected in the Awassa region of Ethiopia before, during and after the 2014-2016 El Niño to quantify the impacts to locally produced food and farmer livelihoods. The collection currently contains soil biophysical and soil water measurements from farmers’ fields in the Halaba district, during the drought and subsequent floods of 2015-2016.
