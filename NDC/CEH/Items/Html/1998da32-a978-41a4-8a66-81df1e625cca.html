Title

Electrical resistivity tomography surveys from the Makutapora Basin, Tanzania, June-July 2019

Description

This datasets contains Electrical Resistivity Tomography surveys taken in the Makutapora Basin, Central Tanzania, using an AGI SuperSting R8 (STING) resistivity meter. Survey geometry, parameters and coordinates are also included.
