Title

Passive sampler ammonia measurements indoors and outdoors at a rural dwelling in South Lanarkshire

Description

This series of datasets consists of ammonia (NH3) measurements in a rural location in South Lanarkshire from a range of years starting in 2017.

Concentrations are measured inside a dwelling, and outside in a garden area surrounded by a grassland which is part of a large dairy farm.
