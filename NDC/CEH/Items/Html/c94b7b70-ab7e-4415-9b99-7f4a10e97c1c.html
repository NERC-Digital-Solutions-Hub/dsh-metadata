Title

Eddy covariance measurements of carbon dioxide, energy and water fluxes at a pasture, Yorkshire, UK, 2021-2023

Description

The dataset includes 30-minute observations of land-atmosphere exchange of net ecosystem carbon dioxide exchange and sensible and latent heat measured over two years in a cut and grazed pasture in Yorkshire, UK. Fluxes were measured using the eddy covariance method.

Fluxes of net ecosystem carbon dioxide exchange, sensible heat, latent heat, atmospheric turbulence and sonic temperature were measured and averaged into 30-minute observations using a LI-COR LI-7200RS enclosed infrared CO2/H2O gas analyser and Gill Windmaster three-dimensional sonic anemometer. Energy fluxes were measured with an Apogee SN-500 net radiometer. Air temperature and relative humidity were measured using a Vaisala HMP155 temperature and humidity probe. Soil temperature and moisture were measured using a METER TEROS 11 temperature and moisture probe.

Data was logged with a Campbell Scientific CR1000X datalogger.

Fluxes were calculated using EddyPro7 V7.0.6 software.

Outliers were removed and gap-filling was done according to a standardized workflow. See the supporting information for more details.
