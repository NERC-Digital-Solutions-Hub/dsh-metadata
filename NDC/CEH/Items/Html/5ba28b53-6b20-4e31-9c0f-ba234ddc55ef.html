Title

Water table depth data from Climoor fieldsite in Clocaenog Forest

Description

This dataset includes manual measurements of water table depth at the Climoor fieldsite in the Clocaenog Forest, north-east Wales. Water table depth was collected via water permeable tubes installed through the soil profile down to bedrock. Measurements were taken, usually every two weeks, using a tape measure and head torch to assist in seeing the water level in the tube. Data are available from May 2009 to January 2014.

Data was written on fieldsheets in the field, then manually entered into the raw data master file on return to the office.
