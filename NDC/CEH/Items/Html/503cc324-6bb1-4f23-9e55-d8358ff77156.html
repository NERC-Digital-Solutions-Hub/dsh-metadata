Title

The Long Term Large Scale Project [LTLS]

Description

Data made available from the Long Term Large Scale (LTLS) project include: Soil survey in England, Scotland and Wales carried out during 2013 and 2014, Survey of bracken biomass, soil chemistry and other environmental factors in Snowdonia and the Lake District in 2014 , Peat survey in England, Scotland and Wales carried out during 2014 [LTLS], Denitrification and greenhouse gas emissions in natural and semi-natural terrestrial ecosystems [LTLS] and Nitrogen and Carbon isotope data from 210Pb dated lake sediment cores in the United Kingdom.

The LTLS project is part of the NERC Macronutrients Cycles Programme and was funded by NERC and the Scottish Government.
