Title

Modelled flood depths and river inflow, with associated rainfall data, in Jakarta for the January-February 2007 flood

Description

The dataset contains model output from the CityCAT hydrodynamic model showing maximum water depths in Jakarta, Indonesia, during the January/February 2007 flood.

The hourly rainfall and hourly lateral inflow boundary conditions from rivers used to obtain the flooding depths are also included.

Lineage

Rainfall data was obtained from hourly and daily measurements with the daily rainfall disaggregated to hourly values. River lateral inflow boundary conditions was obtained from the Shetran hydrological model of the three upstream catchments calibrated against measured discharge data. DEM data in CityCAT was obtained from the aw3d30 DEM dataset and river channels obtained from OpenSteetMap.
