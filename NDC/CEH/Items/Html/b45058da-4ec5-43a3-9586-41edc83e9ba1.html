Title

Soil structure and gas emissions from soil under different management regimes at long-term experimental sites

Description

This dataset contains multiscale soil structure, soil physical properties, greenhouse emissions from soil, and microbial abundance in experimental sites at Rothamsted, UK. Its purpose is to see to what extent, changes in soil structure at various scales due to management effects impact on microbial activity and soil functions.
