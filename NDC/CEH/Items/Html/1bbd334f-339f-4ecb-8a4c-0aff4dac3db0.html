Title

Delivering Food Security on Limited Land (DEVIL)

Description

This series contains data on projections of global changes in water scarcity with the current extent of food products were combined to identify the potential country level vulnerabilities of food product land to water scarcity in 2050.

The data relate to an analysis of the impact changes in water availability will have on food product availability in 2050 for: (i) Croplands (ii) Pasture lands (iii) Maize, rice, wheat, vegetables, pulses and fruit production
