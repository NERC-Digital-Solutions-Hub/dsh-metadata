Title

Peatland distribution maps for lowland Peruvian Amazonia, 2022

Description

Spatial datasets of predicted land cover, peatland extent, peat thickness and peatland carbon storage for the Lowland Peruvian Amazon.

Metadata Date

2025-11-13T16:20:31

Code

Code

Code Space

doi:

Lineage

A total of 1,128 ground reference points of vegetation type, peat presence/absence and (where applicable) peat thickness from the lowland Peruvian Amazon (LPA) were used to train (50% of the data) and validate (the remaining 50% of the data) predictive models of land cover, peatland extent, peat thickness and (in conjunction with laboratory measurements on peat samples) peat carbon storage. Input data to the model included a stack of seven remote-sensing layers, including two Sentinel-2 indices, three ALOS PALSAR-2 bands, SRTM digital elevation data, and a previous land-cover map.
