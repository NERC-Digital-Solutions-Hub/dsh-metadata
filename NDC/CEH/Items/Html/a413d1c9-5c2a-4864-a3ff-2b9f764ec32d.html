Title

Land Cover Map 2022

Description

Land Cover Map 2022 (LCM2022) is a suite of geospatial land cover datasets (raster and polygon) describing the UK land surface in 2022. These were produced at the UK Centre for Ecology & Hydrology by classifying satellite images from 2022.

LCM2022 includes four geospatial datasets each for Great Britain and Northern Ireland: - 10 m Classified Pixel dataset, classified to create a single mosaic of national cover - Land Parcel datasets which are the result of intersecting the 10 m Classified Pixel dataset with the UKCEH Land Parcel Spatial Framework to generate land parcel attributes - 25 m Pixel Rasterised Land Parcel datasets: the result of rasterising the Land Parcel dataset into 25 m pixels - 1 km summary raster products, summarising dominant land cover class and percentage coverage of each land cover class

LCM2022 is the tenth UK land cover map produced by UKCEH. Previous versions are LCM1990, 2000, 2007, 2015, 2017, 2018, 2019, 2020, and 2021.
