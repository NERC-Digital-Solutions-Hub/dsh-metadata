Title

Geochemical characterisation data of sediments and water, and antibiotic profile of water collected along Vashi creek, Mumbai, 2021-2023

Description

This dataset includes a range of determinants present in sediments and water at 8 locations along the Thane River, Vashi Creek, Mumbai between October 2021 and November 2023.
Variables measured include geochemical parameters pH, Ec, OM, MC, LOI, TOC, potentially toxic elements; Al, As, Al, Ca, Cd, Co, Cr, Cu, Fe, Mg, Mn, Ni, Pb, Sn, Se, V, Zn, and anions; fluoride, nitrite, bromide, nitrate, phosphate, and sulphate. Data also includes antibiotic quantification within the Creek over the same time point. Data includes quantity of Azithromycin, Ciprofloxacin, Enrofloxacin, Erythromycin, Levofloxacin, Linezolid, Oxacillin, Penicillin, and Sulfamethazine.

Lineage

Samples were collected in Vashi creek along from a chemical effluent treatment plant (CETP). Surface sediments were grabbed using a Van Veen grab sampler. Water was taken 1 meter above the sediment.

Specific sampling dates: 7/10/21, 8/4/22, 20/01/23, 18/04/2023, 13/11/23

Samples were then screened for antibiotics and geochemical parameters at IIT Bombay, or stabilised and transported to Scotland for metal analysis.

Antibiotics were quantified using an LC-MS orbitrap. Metals quantified using ICP-AES.
