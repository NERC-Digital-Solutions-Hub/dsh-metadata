Title

Chamber flux measurements from Sweden and Finland by 'The Methane and other greenhouse gases in the Arctic: Measurements, process studies and Modelling (MAMM)' project

Description

This data collection consists of static chamber flux measurements for Methane and other greenhouse gases in the Arctic: Measurements, process studies and Modelling (MAMM) project. MAMM aimed to quantify the anthropogenic and natural sources of greenhouse gases – particularly methane – in the Arctic, using data from satellites as well as observations made on the ground and from ships and aircraft. The data in these datasets includes static chamber flux measurements of methane and nitrous oxide, along with associated meteorological and soil measurements.

The MAMM project was part of the UK Natural Environment Research Council funded Arctic Research Programme (ARP), which was launched in 2010 to address specific topics of scientific uncertainty in the Arctic region.
