Title

High-resolution temperature and precipitation measurements for fire-affected locations in Australia (2019-2020) and Tenerife (2020-2021)

Description

This dataset includes temperature and precipitation depth measurements in 10 min intervals taken in 2 locations and time periods after forest fires: - Madre del Agua (Tenerife, Spain): 17/11/20 to 19/11/2021 - Thompson reservoir (Victoria, Australia): 28/03/19 to 13/01/2020 Data was collected using RainWise Rainew raingauges coupled to Onset HOBO pendant dataloggers (UA-003-64) to monitor environmental parameters related to runoff occurrence.

Lineage

Data was collected using RainWise Rainew raingauges coupled to Onset HOBO pendant dataloggers (UA-003-64). The raingauges were installed at 1.5 m over the ground surface and in open areas to avoid precipitation interception by vegetation. Data was downloaded periodically. No data analysis was conducted at this stage.
