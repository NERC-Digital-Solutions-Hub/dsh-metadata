## Title

Long term monitoring data from Loch Leven, Scotland


## Description

A wide range of data have been collected from Loch Leven, a large shallow lake in Scotland, since monitoring began in 1968. These include chemical, biological and physical variables. These data have been collected by the UK Centre for Ecology & Hydrology, including its predecessor bodies, and its collaborative partners.
