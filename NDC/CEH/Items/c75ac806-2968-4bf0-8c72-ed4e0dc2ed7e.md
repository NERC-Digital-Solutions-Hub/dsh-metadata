## Title

Micro-computed tomography (micro-CT) 3D scans of skulls of Passer sparrows generated during a crossing experiment


## Description

This dataset comprises MicroCT 3D scans of skulls from Passer sparrows (Passer domesticus, P. hispaniolensis, P. italiae and F1 hybrids) generated during a crossing experiment at the University of Oslo, Norway in 2014/2015.

Skulls were scanned with a microCT scanner at the University of Nottingham in 2023.

## Lineage

Samples were derived from the collection at the Natural History Museum, Oslo, Norway. All samples were scanned with the use of a high-resolution X-ray Waygate Technologies v|tome|x M 240kV CT system at 26μA at the Hounsfield Facility on the Sutton Bonnington Campus of the University of Nottingham, UK.
