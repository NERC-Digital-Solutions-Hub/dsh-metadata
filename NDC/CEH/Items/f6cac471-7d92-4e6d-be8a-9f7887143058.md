## Title

Grid-to-Grid model estimates of daily mean river flow for gauged catchments in Great Britain: weather@home2 (climate model) driving data [MaRIUS-G2G-WAH2-daily]


## Description

This dataset is a model output, from the Grid-to-Grid hydrological model driven by weather@home2 climate model data. It provides a 100-member ensemble of daily mean river flow (m3/s) for 260 catchments, for the following time periods: historical baseline (HISTBS: 1900-2006), near-future (NF: 2020-2049) and far-future (FF: 2070-2099). It also includes a baseline period (BS: 1975-2005). The catchments correspond to locations of NRFA gauging stations ([http://nrfa.ceh.ac.uk/](http://nrfa.ceh.ac.uk/) 

The data were produced as part of MaRIUS (Managing the Risks, Impacts and Uncertainties of drought and water Scarcity), which was a UK NERC-funded research project (2014-2017) that developed a risk-based approach to drought and water scarcity.

## Lineage

The flow estimates are output from the Grid-to-Grid hydrological model (Bell et al. 2009; [https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jhydrol.2009.08.031](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jhydrol.2009.08.031) The meteorological inputs to the model were the weather@home2 bias-corrected precipitation and potential evaportation (Guillod et al. 2018; [http://dx.doi.org/10.5285/0cea8d7aca57427fae92241348ae9b03](http://dx.doi.org/10.5285/0cea8d7aca57427fae92241348ae9b03) See Supporting Information for more details.
