### Exposed Population Defended (Count)

**Description:**
This field represents the estimated count of the population living within areas protected by flood defences in a given Lower Super Output Area (LSOA). 

**Methodology and Caveats:**
*   **Estimation Method:** This figure provides a broad, area-based indicator of population exposure rather than a precise property-level assessment. It is calculated by attributing 10-meter gridded population estimates (OpenPopGrid, based on 2011 data) into designated flood zones to estimate how many people live within the defended portions of those zones. 
*   **Data Limitations:** This count is an estimate only, as not all defended areas are mapped. For instance, small river and coastal defences, as well as surface water interventions, may not be fully captured in the underlying hazard data. Additionally, the recorded defences generally relate to river and coastal flooding only, rather than surface water.
