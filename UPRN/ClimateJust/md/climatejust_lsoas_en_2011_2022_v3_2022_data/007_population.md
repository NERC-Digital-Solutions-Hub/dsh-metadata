### Population

**Description:**
This field represents the total population count for a given Lower Super Output Area (LSOA) in England. It serves as the baseline demographic figure used to assess the scale of potential exposure within a neighbourhood. When combined with hazard data (such as the estimated count of the population living within a flood zone), this total population figure helps to determine the overall climate disadvantage of the area.

**Data Source:**
The data is derived from the 2011 UK Census, serving as a confirmed match for the population baseline used in the calculations. For context, an average LSOA in England contains a population of approximately 1,600 people.
