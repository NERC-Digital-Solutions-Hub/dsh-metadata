### Exposed Population Undefended (Count)

**Description:**
This field represents the estimated count of the population living within designated flood zones that are *not* protected by recorded flood defences in a given Lower Super Output Area (LSOA). 

**Methodology and Caveats:**
*   **Estimation Method:** This figure provides a broad, area-based indicator of population exposure rather than a precise property-level assessment. It is calculated by attributing 10-meter gridded population estimates (OpenPopGrid, based on 2011 data) into designated flood zones to estimate how many people live within the undefended portions of those zones. 
*   **Data Limitations:** This count is an estimate only. Because not all defended areas are mapped (such as small river and coastal defences, as well as surface water interventions), some populations counted as "undefended" may actually benefit from unmapped local protections. Additionally, the underlying data for recorded defences generally relates only to river and coastal flooding, rather than surface water flooding.
