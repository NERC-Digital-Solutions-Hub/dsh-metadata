
## 'All pensioner households (%)'

The field **'All pensioner households (%)'** (internal field name `AllPensioner_HH`) represents the percentage of households within a neighborhood where all residents are of pensionable age.

### **Description**

In the context of the **UK Social Flood Risk and Vulnerability** dataset, this field serves as a proxy indicator for **income-related vulnerability**. It identifies communities that may have more limited financial resources, which can directly reduce their **ability to prepare for, respond to, and recover from** flooding events.

* **Assumption:** Households consisting entirely of pensioners tend to have lower incomes compared to working households, leading to higher social vulnerability.
* **Vulnerability Dimension:** This metric informs the **Ability to Prepare**, **Ability to Respond**, and **Ability to Recover** indices within the Neighbourhood Flood Vulnerability Index (NFVI) framework.
* **Data Source:** Derived from the **2011 Census** (Table QS113).
