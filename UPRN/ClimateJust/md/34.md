**(In)ability to Recover** - A person’s or community’s ability to recover from climate and extreme weather events is governed primarily by social factors. With respect to flood this includes factors such as social networks, mobility and the availability of services. The map shows the result of an equally-weighted combination of neighbourhood-level scores for indicators within the domains associated with respect to floods:

- Income
- Language
- Internet
- Social networks
- Mobility
- Pharmacy access
- House prices
