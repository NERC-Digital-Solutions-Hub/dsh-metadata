(In)ability to Prepare - A person’s or community’s ability to prepare for climate and extreme weather events is governed primarily by social factors. With respect to heat this includes factors such as income and tenure. The map shows the result of an equally-weighted combination of neighbourhood-level scores for indicators within the domains associated with respect to heat:

  • Income
  • Tenure
  • Language
  • Internet
  • Local knowledge

    • The calculated Inability to Prepare Index Z-Score is plotted using the following classification scheme:

    Z-scoreLabelColour
    ≥ 2.5Acute
    1.5 to 2.5Extremely high
    0.5 to 1.5Relatively high
    0.5 to -0.5Average
    -0.5 to -1.5Relatively low
    -1.5 to -2.5Extremely low
    ≤ - 2.5Slight
